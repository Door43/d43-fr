=====Questions de Compréhension et Réponses:=====

  - **What question did Peter ask Jesus?** \\ **Quelle question Pierre a t-il posé à Jésus?**
    *//"How many times should I forgive my brother when he sins against me?" [29-01]//
    *//Combien de fois  pardonnerai-je mon frère quand il péche contre moi? Pierre demanda à Jésus.[29-01]//
  - **How many times did Peter think he should forgive his brother?** \\ **Combien de fois Pierre pensait-il qu'il devrait pardonner son frère?** 
    *//Seven times. [29-01]//
    *//7 fois. [29-01]//
  - **How many times did Jesus say he should forgive his brother?** \\ **Combien de fois Jésus a dit qu'il devrait pardonner son frère?** 
    *//Seventy times seven. [29-01]//
    *//70 fois.[29-01]//
  - **What did Jesus mean when he said, "Seventy times seven?** \\ **Qu'est-ce que Jésus voulait dire quand il disait "70 fois?** 
    *//He meant that we should always forgive. [29-01]//
    *//On devrait toujours pardonner[29-01].//
  - **	Was the servant able to pay his huge debt to the king?** \\ **Est-ce que le serviteur pouvait payer sa dette énorme au roi?**
    *//No. [29-03]//
    *//Non.[29-03]//
  - **How did the king punish the servant with the huge debt?** \\ **Comment le roi a t-il puni le serviteur qui avait une énorme dette?**
    *//The king ordered that the man and his family be sold as slaves. [29-03]//
    *//Le roi ordonna que l'on vende comme esclaves l'homme et sa famille.[29-03]// 
  - **What did the king do when the servant begged him to be patient?** \\ **Qu'a fait le roi quand le serviteur l'a supplié d'être patient?**
    *//The king felt pity on the servant and cancelled his debt. [29-04]//
    *//Il a eu pitié du serviteur et a annulé sa dette.[29-04]//
  - **What did the servant then do to a fellow servant who owed him a small debt?** \\ ** Puis qu'a fait  le serviteur envers un autre qui lui devait un petite dette?**

  - **What did the servant do when his fellow servant fell on his knees and asked him to be patient?**
    *//He threw his fellow servant into prison. [29-06]//
  - **How did the other servants react when he threw his fellow servant in prison?**
    *//They were greatly disturbed and told the king about it. [29-07]//
  - **How did the king punish the servant who did not forgive his fellow servant?**
    *//He threw him into prison. [29-08]//
