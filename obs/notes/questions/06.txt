====== Story #6: God Provides for Isaac: Dieu a donne Isaac ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers : Questions de Comprehension et Reponses =====

  - **Why did Abraham send his servant back to the land of Abraham's relatives?** \\  **Pourquoi Abraham a t-il envoye son serviteur dans pays des le membres de sa famille**
    * //To bring back a wife for his son, Isaac. [06-01]// \\ //De ramener une femme for son flls Isaak//
  - **How did the servant find Rebekah?** \\ **Comment le serviteur a trouve Rebeca**
    * //God led him to her. [06-02]// \\ //Dieu la lui a laissee//
  - **How was Rebekah related to Abraham?** \\ **Quelle a ete la relation entre Rebeca et Abraham**
    * //Rebekah was the granddaughter of Abraham's brother. [06-02] \\ Rebeca a ete  petite fille du frere d'Abraham//
  - **Was Rebekah forced to go with the servant to marry Isaac? \\ Rebeca avait-elle ete forcee de partir avec le serviteur  et de se marier avec Isaac**
    * //No, she agreed to go. [06-03] \\ marier isaak :No,elle avait  consenti de partir//
  - **What promises of God were passed on to Isaac when Abraham died?** \\ **quelles ont ete les peomesses de Dieu pour Isaac quand Abraham etait decede**
    * //All of the promises that God had made to Abraham, including that he would have countless descendants. [06-04]// \\ //Toutes les promesses que Dieu avait faites a Abraham, y compris les mutitudes promesses faites aux  descendants//
  - **Why did it appear that the promise of countless descendants would not be fulfilled through Isaac?** \\ **Pourquoi  il apparait que la promesse faite aux multitudes des  descendants n'ont pas ete accomplies a travers Isaac**
    * //Rebekah could not have children. [06-04] \\ Rebeca  ne doit pas avoir d'enfants//
  - **What did God tell Rebekah about her two twin sons before they were born? \\ Qu'est ce que Dieu a dit a Rebeca au sujet de ses deux fils  jumeaux  avant qu'ils soient nes**
    * //They would become two nations, and the older son will serve the younger one. [06-06]// \\ // ils deviendront deux nations, et le plus grand enfant servira le plus petit//
  - **What was the name of the older son? \\ Quel est le nom du plus grand**
    * //Esau. [06-07] \\ Esau//
  - **What was the name of the younger son? \\ quel est le nom du plus enfant**
    * //Jacob. [06-07] \\ Jacob//
