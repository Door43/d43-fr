====== Story #4: God's Covenant with Abraham:  Alliance de Dieu avec Abraham ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers : des questions de conprehension avec reponses =====

  - **After the flood, did the people fill the earth as God had commanded?**
  -        * **//Apres le deluge le peuple avait  -il  rempli la terre comme Dieu avait recommande// **
      * //No. They gathered together and built a city. [04-01]//
      * //Non ils  s'etaient **assemble pour construire une ville** //
  - **How many different languages were there in the world at that time?**
  - **combien de differentes langues existaient 'elles en ce temps**
      * //There was only one language. [04-01]//
      * **//Il y avait une langue// **
  - **What did the people work together to do instead of filling the earth?**
  - **Qu"est ce que le peuple ensemble avait fait au lieu de remplir la terre**
      * //The started to build a tall tower to reach heaven. [04-02]//
      * Ils ont **commence a ** construire une haute tour pour atteindre le ciel
  - **What did God do to cause the people to spread out all over the world?**
  - **Qu'est ce  que Dieu a  fait pour  amener les gens a s'eparpiller dans le monde**
      * //He changed their language into many different languages. [04-03]//
      * //Il a change leur language  en **plusieurs differentes langues** //
  - **What was the name of the city that they were building?**
  -        * **//Quel a ete le nom de la ville qu'ils avaient construite// **
      * //Babel. [04-03]//
      * **//Babel// **
  - **What does the name "Babel" mean?**
  - **qu'est ceque le nom Babel signifie**
      * //Confused. [04-03]//
      * **//Confus// **
  - **What did God tell Abram to do?**
  - **Qu'est ce que Dieu a dit a Abram de faire**
      * //God told Abram to leave his country and family and go to another land. [04-04]//
      * Dieu a dit **a Abram de queitter son pays et sa famille et de partir dans un autre  pays**
  - **What did God promise to do for Abram?**
  - // **Qu'est ce que Dieu a promis a Abram de faire**//      * //He promised to give Abram all the land he could see, to make his name great, to make his descendants a great nation, and to bless all the families on earth through him. [04-04]//
      * **//Il a promis de donner a Abram touts les pays qu'il verra, de rendre grand son nom, de ses descendant , une grande nation, de benir toutes les familles de la terre par lui // **
  - **To which land did Abram go?**
      * //The land of Canaan. [04-05]//
  - **What promise did God make to Abram about Canaan?**
      * //God would give it to Abram and his descendants as an inheritance. [04-06]//
  - **Who was Melchizedek?**
      * //He was the priest of God Most High. [04-07]//
  - **What did Melchizedek do for Abram?**
      * //He blessed Abram. [04-07]//
  - **What did Abram give to Melchizedek?**
      * //A tenth of all he owned. [04-07]//
  - **What did God promise Abram after he had lived in Canaan for many years?**
      * //Abram would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. [04-08]//
  - **Why did God say Abram was righteous?**
      * //Because Abram believed God's promise. [04-08]//
  - **What is a covenant?**
      * //An agreement between two parties. [04-09]//
