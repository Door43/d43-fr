===== Jacob Went to Egypt [08-14] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-14.jpg?nolink&}}

**Even though Jacob was an old man, he moved to Egypt** with all of his family, and they all lived there. **Before Jacob died**, he **blessed** each of his sons.

Quoique Jacob fut très âgé, Il alla vivre en Égypte avec toute sa famille. Avant de mourir, Jacob bénit chacun des ses fils.

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:jacob|Jacob (Jacob)]]**

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **Even though Jacob was an old man, he moved to Egypt**  - Egypt was a great distance away from Canaan, and it would have been difficult for an elderly man to walk or ride a cart that far.
  * **Before Jacob died**  - Jacob died in Egypt. He did not get to return to Canaan, the land God had promised to give to him and his descendants.
==== notes de traduction: ====

* Même si Jacob était un vieil homme, il se installe à l'Egypte - L'Egypte était une grande distance de Canaan, et il aurait été difficile pour un homme âgé de marcher ou monter un chariot aussi loin.

* Avant mort Jacob - Jacob est mort en Egypte. Il n'a pas pour retourner à Canaan, la terre que Dieu avait promis de donner à lui et ses descendants.
