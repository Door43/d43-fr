===== Jacob Prospered - Jacob Prospéra F [07-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jacob** lived with **Rebekah's** relatives for many years. During that time he married and had twelve sons and a daughter. **God** made him very **wealthy**.

**Jacob** vécut avec la famille de **Rebecca** pendant plusieurs années durant lesquelles il se maria et eut douze fils et une fille. **Dieu** le rendit très **riche**.

=====Translation Notes=====
 
  * **wealthy** – This included money, large herds of animals and other possessions.
  

===== Note de traduction: =====

  * **riche** - Cela comprenait de l'argent, de grands troupeaux d'animaux et d'autres biens.
