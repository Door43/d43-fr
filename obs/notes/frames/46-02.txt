===== Saul Saw Jesus  Saul A Vu Jésus [46-02] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-02.jpg?nolink&}}

While **Saul** was **on his way** to Damascus, a bright light from heaven shone all around him, and he fell to the ground. Saul heard someone say, “Saul! Saul! Why do you **persecute** me?” Saul asked, “Who are you, **Master**?” Jesus replied to him, “I am Jesus. You are persecuting me!”

Pendant que **Saul** était **en route vers** Damas, une lumière brillante apparut du ciel tout autour de lui et il tomba a terre. Saul entendit quelqu'un dire, “Saul! Saul! Pourquoi tu me **persécutes**?” Saul demanda, “qui êtes-vous **Maitre**?” Jésus lui répondit, “Je suis Jésus que tu persécutes.”
