===== Jesus Walked with the Disciples / Les Disciples Ont Marché Avec Jésus [42-02] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** approached them and started walking with them, but they **did not recognize him**. He asked what they were talking about, and they told him about **all the remarkable things that had happened** concerning Jesus during the previous few days. They thought they were talking to a visitor who did not know what had happened in **Jerusalem**.

**Jésus** s'approcha d'eux et commenca à marcher avec eux, mais ils **ne l'ont pas reconnu**. Il leur dit: “De quoi discutiez vous en marchant?”. Ils lui racontèrent **toutes les choses remarquables qui s'étaient passées** ces derniers jours concernant Jésus. Ils pensaient qu'ils parlaient à un étranger qui ne savait pas ce qui s'était passé à Jérusalem.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **did not recognize him**  - That is, "did not know that it was Jesus."
  * **all the remarkable things that had happened**  - This refers to the teaching and miracles of Jesus, his death, and the reports of his resurrection.
===== Notes de traduction: =====

  * **ne l'ont pas reconnu**  - C'est à dire, "ne savaient pas que c'était Jésus."
  * **toutes les choses remarquables qui s'étaient passées**  - Il s'agit de l'enseignement et des miracles de Jésus, sa mort, et les comptes rendus sur sa résurrection.
