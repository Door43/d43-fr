===== Jesus Walked with the Disciples [42-02] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** approached them and started walking with them, but they **did not recognize him**. He asked what they were talking about, and they told him about **all the remarkable things that had happened** concerning Jesus during the previous few days. They thought they were talking to a visitor who did not know what had happened in **Jerusalem**.

Jésus s'approcha d'eux et commenca à marcher avec eux, mais leurs yeux étaient incapables de le reconnaître. Il leur dit: “De quoi discutiez vous en marchant?”. Ils lui racontèrent toutes les choses remarquables qui s'étaient passées ces derniers jours concernant Jésus. Les disciples pensaient que cet homme était un étranger qui ne savait pas ce qui s'était passé à Jérusalem.

===== Important Terms: =====


  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms/jesus|Jesus]]**
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms/jerusalem|Jerusalem]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **did not recognize him**  - That is, "did not know that it was Jesus."
  * **all the remarkable things that had happened**  - This refers to the teaching and miracles of Jesus, his death, and the reports of his resurrection.
===== Note de traduction: =====

  * ** Ne le connaissais pas ** - Ce est, "ne savait pas que ce était Jésus."
  * ** Toutes les choses remarquables qui se était passé ** - Il se agit de l'enseignement et les miracles de Jésus, sa mort, et les rapports de sa résurrection.
