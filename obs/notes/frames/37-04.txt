===== Martha Met Jesus / Marthe A Rencontré Jésus [37-04] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Jesus** arrived at **Lazarus**' hometown, Lazarus had already been **dead** for four days. **Martha went out to meet Jesus** and said, “**Master**, if only you had been here, **my brother would not have died**. But I **believe God will give you whatever you ask from him**.”

Quand **Jésus** arriva dans la ville natale de **Lazare**, Lazare était déjà **mort** depuis quatre jours. **Marthe alla à la rencontre de Jésus** et dit “**Seigneur**, si tu avais été là, **mon frère ne serait pas mort**. Mais je **crois que Dieu t'accordera tout ce que tu lui demanderas**.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Martha**  - Martha was the sister of Lazarus and Mary. See **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]**.
  * **went out to meet Jesus**  - That is, "went to meet Jesus as he was coming into the town."
  * **my brother would not have died.**  - That is, "you would have healed my brother and he would not have died" or, "you could have prevented my brother from dying."
  * **will give you whatever you ask from him**  - That is, "will do whatever you ask him to do."
===== Notes de traduction: =====

  * **Marthe**  - Marthe était la sœur de Lazare et de Marie. Voir **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]**.
  * **Marthe alla à la rencontre de Jésus**  - C'est à dire, "est allé à la rencontre de Jésus alors qu'il entrait dans la ville"
  * **Mon frère ne serait pas mort**  - C'est à dire , "vous auriez guéri mon frère et il ne serait pas mort", ou "vous auriez pu empêcher mon frère de mourir."
  * **t'accordera tout ce que tu lui demanderas**  - C'est à dire, "fera ce que tu lui demanderas de faire."
