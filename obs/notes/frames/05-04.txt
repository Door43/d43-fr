===== God Promised Isaac's Birth [05-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?nolink&}}

“Your wife, **Sarai**, will have a son—he will be the **son** of **promise**. Name him **Isaac**. **I will make my covenant with him**, and he will become a great nation.

“Ta femme, Sarai, aura un fils, il sera le fils de la Promesse. Tu lui donneras le nom d'Isaac. J'établirai mon Alliance avec lui et il deviendra une grande nation.

I will make **Ismael** a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac.” Then **God** changed **Abram's** name to **Abraham**, which means “**father of many**.” God also changed Sarai's name to **Sarah**, which means “**princess**.”

. Je ferai d'Ishmaël une grande nation aussi, mais mon Alliance sera avec Isaac.” Puis Dieu changea le nom d'Abram qui devint Abraham, ce qui signifie “père de la multitude.” Dieu changea aussi le nom de Sarai en Sara, qui veut dire “princesse.”

===== Translation Notes: Notes de traduction: =====

  * (God continues to speak to Abram.)
  * **son of promise**  - Isaac would be the son that God promised to Sarai and Abram. He would also be the son that God would use to give Abram many descendants.
  * **fils de la promesse **- Isaac serait le fils que Dieu a promis à Abram et Saraï. Il serait également le fils que Dieu a utilisé pour donner à Abram de nombreux descendants.
  * **I will make my covenant with him**  - This would be the same covenant that God made with Abram.
  * Je établirai mon alliance avec lui - Ce serait la même alliance que Dieu a fait avec Abram.
  * **father of many**  - As God had promised, Abraham would become the ancestor of many people who would even become many nations.

  * **princess**  - A princess is a daughter of a king. The names Sarai and Sarah apparently both meant "princess." But God changed her name to signify that she would be the mother of many nations, and some of her descendants would be kings.
  * **princesse**
- Une princesse est une fille du roi. Les deux noms Sarai et Sara apparemment signifient “princesse.” Mais Dieu a changé le nom de Sara pour signifier qu'elle serait la mère de plusieurs nations, et certains de ses descendants seraient des rois.
