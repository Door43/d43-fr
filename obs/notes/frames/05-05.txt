===== Birth of Isaac - Naissance d'Isaac F [05-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg?nolink&}}

That day **Abraham circumcised all the males in his household**. About a year later, when Abraham was 100 years old and **Sarah** was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's **son**. They named him **Isaac** as **God** had told them to do.

Ce jour-là Abraham circoncit tous les mâles de sa maison. Un an plus tard, alors qu'Abraham avait 100 ans et Sarah 90 ans, Sara enfanta le fils d'Abraham. Ils lui donnèrent le nom d'Isaac selon l'ordre que Dieu lui avait donné.

=====Translation Notes=====
  * **all the males in his household**  - This means all of the men and boys that Abraham was responsible for, including his male servants, young and old.


===== Note de traduction: =====

  * **tous les mâles de sa maison** - Cela signifie tous les hommes et les garçons dont Abraham était responsable, y compris ses serviteurs, jeunes et vieux.
