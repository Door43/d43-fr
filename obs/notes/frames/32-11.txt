===== Go Home [32-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-11.jpg?nolink&}}

But **Jesus** said to him, “No, I want you to go home and tell your friends and family about everything that **God** has done for you and **how he has had mercy on you**.”

Mais Jésus lui dit: “Non, je veux que tu ailles dans ta maison, et que tu racontes à tes amis et ta famille tout ce que le Seigneur a fait pour toi, et comment il a eu pitié de toi.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms/jesus|Jesus]]**
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms/god|God]]**
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms/mercy|mercy]] on you**."


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **how he has had mercy on you**  - This can be translated as, "how he has shown mercy to you" or, "that he has been merciful to you."
===== Note de traduction: =====

* Comment il a eu pitié de toi - Cela peut être traduit comme "comment il a montré la miséricorde de vous» ou «qu'il a été miséricordieux envers vous."
