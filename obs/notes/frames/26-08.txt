===== Jesus Healed the Sick / Jésus Guérit le Malade [26-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus went throughout the region of Galilee**, and large crowds came to him. They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus **healed** them.

Puis Jésus s'en alla dans la région de Galilée, et des grandes foules le suivirent. Elles amenèrent les malades ou infirmes, y compris ceux qui ne pouvaient pas voir, marcher, entendre ou parler, et Jésus les guérit.


===== Termes Importants =====
  * **[[:fr:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
  * **[[:fr:obe:other:galilee|Galilee, Galilean]]**
  * **[[:fr:obe:other:heal|heal, cure]]**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **went throughout the region of Galilee**  - That is, "all around in Galilee" or, "from place to place in Galilee."
===== Note de traduction: =====

  * **Allés dans toute la région de Galilée**  - C'est à dire, "tout autour de la  Galilée» ou "d'un endroit à l'autre en Galilée."
