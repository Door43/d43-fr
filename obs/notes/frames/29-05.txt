===== The Small Debt [29-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-05.jpg?nolink&}}

“But when the **servant** went out from the king, he found a **fellow servant** who owed him a debt worth four months’ wages. The servant grabbed his fellow servant and said, ‘Pay me the money that you owe me!’”

“Mais quand le serviteur quitta le roi, il rencontra un autre serviteur qui avait envers lui une dette équivalente à plusieurs mois de salaire. Le premier serviteur saisit l'homme et lui dit: “Rends moi ce que tu me dois.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * **fellow servant**  - This refers to another man who was also a servant of the king.
===== Note de traduction: =====

  * (Jésus continue l'histoire.)
  * **Compagnon de service** - Il se agit à un autre homme qui était aussi un serviteur du roi.
