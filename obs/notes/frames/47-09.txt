===== The Earthquake / Le tremblement de terre [47-09] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Suddenly, there was a violent earthquake! All the prison doors **flew open**, and the chains of all the prisoners **fell off**.

Soudainement, il y eut un violent tremblement. Toutes les portes de la prison **s'ouvrirent** et les chaînes de tous les prisonniers **se détachèrent**.

===== Termes Importants =====
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **flew open**  - That is, "became unlocked and suddenly opened wide."
  * **fell off**  - That is, "suddenly came off" or, "suddenly came apart so that the prisoners were released."
===== Notes de traduction: =====

  * **s'ouvrirent**  - Cela signifie, "se déverrouillèrent et s'ouvrirent."
  * **se détachèrent**  - Cela signifie," "se délièrent" ou "se rompirent soudainement de sorte que les prisoniers étaient libres."
