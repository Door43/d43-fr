===== The Water Became Blood / L'Eau se transforma en sang [10-03] F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-03.jpg?nolink&}}

**God turned the Nile River into blood**, but **Pharaoh** still would not let the **Israelites** go.

**Dieu transforma le Nil en sang**, mais **Pharaon** continua à ne pas vouloir laisser partir les **Israélites**.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **turned the Nile River into blood**  - Some languages may need to say, "turned the water in the Nile River into blood." Instead of water in the river there was blood, so the fish died and the people had no water to drink.
===== Note de Traduction: =====

  * **transforma le Nil en sang**  - Certaines langues pourraient dire, "a transformé l'eau du Nil" en sang. A la place de l'eau dans le fleuve, il y avait du sang de sorte que les poissons mourraient et les gens n'avaient pas d'eau à boire.
