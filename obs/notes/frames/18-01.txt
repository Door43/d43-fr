===== God Made Solomon Wise [18-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-01.jpg?nolink&}}

After many years, **David** died, and his son **Solomon** began to rule over **Israel**. **God** spoke to Solomon and asked him what he wanted most. When Solomon **asked for wisdom**, God was pleased and made him the wisest man in the world. Solomon learned many things and was a very wise **judge**. God also made him very wealthy.

De nombreuses années plus tard, David mourut et son fils Salomon commença à régner sur Israël. Dieu parla à Salomon et lui demanda ce qu'il désirait le plus. Quand Salomon demanda de la sagesse, Dieu fut content et fit de lui l'homme le plus sage au monde. Salomon apprit beaucoup de choses et il fut un juge très sage. Dieu fit de lui aussi un homme très riche.
