===== Isaac Blessed Jacob - Isaac Bénit Jacob F [07-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jacob came to Isaac** and said, “I am **Esau**. I have come so that you can **bless** me.” When Isaac felt the goat hair and smelled the clothes, **he thought it was Esau** and blessed him.

**Jacob alla voir Isaac** et lui dit, “Je suis **Esau**. Je suis venu pour que tu me **bénisses**.” Quand Isaac toucha la peau de chevreau et prit l'odeur des vêtements, **Il crut que c'était Esau** et il le bénit.

=====Translation Notes=====
  * **Jacob came to Isaac** - In some languages it might be more natural to say, "Jacob went to Isaac."
  * **he thought it was Esau**  - He thought that the person he was touching and smelling was Esau.


===== Note de traduction: =====

  * **Jacob arriva devant Isaac** -  Dans certaines langues, il pourrait être plus naturel de dire, "Jacob alla vers Isaac."
  * **Il pensa que c'était Esau** - Il pensa que la personne qu'il touchait et sentait était Esaü.
