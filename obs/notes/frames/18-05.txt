===== Rehoboam Became King [18-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg?nolink&}}

After **Solomon** died, his son, **Rehoboam**, became **king**. Rehoboam was a foolish man. All the people of the **nation of Israel** came together to **confirm him as king**. They complained to Rehoboam that Solomon had made them do a lot of hard work and pay a lot of taxes.

Après la mort de Salomon, son fils, Réhoboam, devint roi. Réhoboam était un homme stupide. Tous les habitants de la nation d'Israël se mirent ensemble pour le confirmer en tant que roi. Ils se plaignirent à Réhoboam que Salomon les fit travailler très dur et payer beaucoup de taxes.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **confirm him as king**  - That is, "tell him that they were glad he was king and that they would do what he said."
==== notes de traduction: ====

* Le confirmer comme roi - Ce est, "lui dire qu'ils étaient heureux qu'il était roi et qu'ils feraient ce qu'il a dit."
