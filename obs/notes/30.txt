====== 30. Jesus Feeds Five Thousand People  Jésus A Nourri 5000 personnes ======

===== Overview: =====

Jesus went with his disciples to a quiet place to rest, but many people followed them. So he taught them and healed their sick. When they became hungry, there were only two loaves of bread and two fish for five thousand people. However, Jesus blessed the food and told his disciples to give it to the people. Everyone had enough to eat, and there was even food left over.

Jésus se retira avec ses disciples dans un endroit paisible pour se reposer, mais beaucoup de gens le suivirent. Alors Il les enseigna et les guérit leurs malades. Quand ils eurent faim, il n'y avait que deux pains et deux poissons pour 5000 personnes. Cependant Jésus bénit la nourriture et dit à ses disciples de la donner aux gens. Chacun eut assez et il y eut même beaucoup de nourriture restante.
