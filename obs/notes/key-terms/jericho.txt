====== Jericho Jericho ======

===== Facts:  Les faits =====

Jericho was a powerful city in the Promised Land of Canaan.

Jericho était une ville puissante dans la terre promise de Canaan.

  * This was the first city in the land of Canaan that God told the Israelites to conquer.
  * C'etait la premiere ville dans la terre promise de Canaan que Dieu a dit aux Israelites de conquerir.
  * Like all the Canaanites, the people of Jericho worshiped false idols.
  * Comme tous les Canaaneens, les gens de Jericho ont adoré de faux dieux.
  * When Joshua led the Israelites against Jericho, God did a great miracle to help them defeat the city.
  * Quand Josué a guidé les Israélites contre Jéricho, Dieu a fait un grand miracle pour les aider à vaincre la ville.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-01|[15-01]]]** Joshua sent two spies to the Canaanite city of **Jericho**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-03|[15-03]]]** After the people crossed the Jordan River, God told Joshua how to attack the powerful city of **Jericho**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-05|[15-05]]]** Then the walls around **Jericho**  fell down! The Israelites destroyed everything in the city as God had commanded.
