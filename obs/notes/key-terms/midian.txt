====== Midian:Medean ======

==== (Midian, Midianites) Median Medianite ====

===== Facts: les faits =====

Midian was a people group located in the northern Arabian Desert to the south of the land of Canaan. The people of that group were called "Midianites."

Median est un groupe de personnes installe dans le nord du Desert arabique et au sud du territoirede Canaan Le peuple de ce groupe est appele Medianites

  * Midian existed from around the 19th to the 11th centuries B.C.
  * Midian a existe au tour du 19 e siecle au 11 e siecle Av J,c,
  * Joseph was taken to Egypt by a group of Midianite slave traders.
  * Joseph a ete pris en Egypte  par un groupe de medianites commercants
  * Many years later the Midianites attacked and raided the Israelites in the land of Canaan. Gideon led the Israelites in defeating them.
  * Plusieurs annees avant les medianites attaquaint et envoye des raids dans le camp des canaaniens
  * Many of the modern-day Arabian tribes are descendants of this group.
  * Plusieurs tributs arabes a nos jours sont des descendands de ce groupe
