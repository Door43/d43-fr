====== praise: Louer   F ======

==== (praise, to praise) : louange , Louer ====

===== Definition: Définition =====

To praise someone is to express admiration and honor for that person.

Louer quelqu'un, c'est exprimer de l'admiration et de l'honneur envers cette personne.

  * People praise God because of how great he is and because of all the amazing things he has done as the Creator and Savior of the world.
  * Le peuple loue Dieu à cause de sa grandeur et de toutes les choses magnifiques qu'Il a faites en tant que Créateur et Sauveur du monde.
  * Praise for God often includes being thankful for what he has done.
  * Louer Dieu inclut souvent les actions de grace pour toutes les choses qu'Il a faites
  * Music and singing is often used as a way to praise God.
  * La musique et les chants sont souvent utisés comme une maniere de louer Dieu
  * "Praise" that is directed to God is often a part of "worship."
  * Les louanges qui sont dirigées vers Dieu font souvent partie de l'adoration.

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:22-07|[22-07]]]** Zechariah said, "**Praise**  God, because he has remembered his people!
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:43-13|[43-13]]]** They (disciples) enjoyed **praising**  God together and they shared everything they had with each other.
