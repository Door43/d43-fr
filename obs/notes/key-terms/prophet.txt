====== prophet : prophète  F ======

==== (prophet, prophecy, prophesy) : prophète, prophétie, prophétiser ====

===== Definition: Définition =====

A prophet is a person who speaks God's messages to people. He tells them what God wants them to know. God is the one who chooses and appoints the prophet to do this.

Un prophète est une personne qui déclare les message de Dieu au peuple. Il leur dit ce que Dieu veut qu'il sache. Dieu est celui qui choisit et désigne les prophètes pour faire cela.

  * "Prophet" could be translated as "God's spokesman."
  * Le terme "Prophète" peut être traduit par "le Porte-Parole de Dieu".
  * A "prophecy" is the message that God tells the prophet to speak.
  * Une prophétie est un message que Dieu dit au le prophète de declarer.
  * To "prophesy" is the act of communicating God's message.
  * Prophétiser, c'est l'action de communiquer le message de Dieu.
  * Prophets told people about things that God had said would happen in the future.
  * Les prophètes annoncent au peuple les choses que Dieu avait dit allaient se réaliser dans le futur.
  * Prophets also warned people to turn away from their sins and obey God.Les
  * Les prophètes disent aussi aux gens de se détourner de leus péchés et d'obéir à Dieu.
  * There were also false prophets who claimed to speak messages from false gods such as Baal.
  * Il y avait aussi des faux prophètes qui prétendaient donner des messages de leurs faux dieu tels que Baal.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-12|[12-12]]]** When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they trusted in God and believed that Moses was a **prophet**  of God.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the **prophet**  Nathan to tell David how evil his sin was.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:19-01|[19-01]]]** Throughout the history of the Israelites, God sent them **prophets**. The **prophets**  heard messages from God and then told the people God's messages.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:19-06|[19-06]]]** All the people of the entire kingdom of Israel, including the 450 **prophets**  of Baal, came to Mount Carmel.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:19-17|[19-17]]]** Most of the time, the people did not obey God. They often mistreated the **prophets**  and sometimes even killed them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The **prophet**  Isaiah **prophesied**  that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:43-05|[43-05]]]**"This fulfills the **prophecy**  made by the **prophet**  Joel in which God said, 'In the last days, I will pour out my Spirit.'"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:43-07|[43-07]]]**"This fulfills the **prophecy**  which says, 'You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave.'"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-12|[48-12]]]** Moses was a great **prophet**  who proclaimed the word of God. But Jesus is the greatest **prophet**  of all. He is the Word of God.
