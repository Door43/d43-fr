====== John (the Baptist)  Jean (le Baptiste) ======

===== Facts: Les faits =====

John was the son of Zechariah and Elizabeth. Since "John" was a common name, he is often called "John the Baptist" to distinguish him from the other people named John, such as the Apostle John.

Jean etait le fils de Zacharie et d'Elizabeth. Etant donné que "Jean" etait un nom commun, il est souvent appelé "Jean le Baptiste" pour le distinguer d'autres individus nommés Jean, tel que l'apotre Jean.

  * John was the prophet whom God sent to prepare people to believe in and follow the Messiah.
  * Jean etait le prophete que Dieu a envoye pour preparer les gens a croire et a suivre le Messie.
  * John told people to confess their sins, turn to God, and stop sinning, so that they would be ready to receive the Messiah.
  * Jean a dit aux gens de confesser leurs peches, de se tourner vers Dieu et de cesser de pecher, de maniere a ce qu'ils soient prets a recevoir le Messie.
  * John baptized many people in water as a sign that they were sorry for their sins and were turning away from them.
  * Jean a baptisé beaucoup de gens dans de l'eau comme signe qu'ils etaient desoles pour leur peches et se detournaient d'eux.
  * John was called "John the Baptist" because he baptized many people.
  * Jean etait appelé "Jean le Baptiste" parce qu'il baptisait beaucoup de personnes.
  * Make sure that people will understand that "the Baptist" does not refer to the church by this name. It may be necessary to say instead, "John the Baptizer" or, "John who baptizes."
  * Assurez vous que les gens vont comprendre que "le Baptiste" ne se refere pas à l'eglise par son nom. Il serait peut-etre necessaire de dire "Jean le Baptiseur" ou, "Jean qui baptise".
