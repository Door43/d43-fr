====== altar autel ======

===== Definition: Définition: =====

An altar is a raised structure on which sacrifices were burned as gifts, or offerings, to God.

Un autel est une structure surélevée sur laquelle des sacrifices étaient brulés comme cadeaux, ou dons à Dieu.

  * Altars were often made by packing together a mound of earth or by carefully placing stones into a large pile.
  * Des autels étaient souvent fabriqués en rassemblant une butte de terre ou en disposant soigneusement des pierres dans une grande pile.
  * Some special altars were made of metal, such as gold or bronze.
  * Certains autels speciaux etaient fabriqués avec du metal, de l'or ou du bronze.
  * God told his people to build altars on which to make sacrifices to him, and the nations around Israel also built altars to make sacrifices to their gods.
  * Dieu a dit a son peuple de construire des autels sur lesquels faire des sacrifices a son honneur, et les nations autour d'Israel ont aussi construit des autels pour faire des sacrifices a leur dieux

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:03-14|[03-14]]]** After Noah got off the boat, he built an **altar**  and sacrificed some of each kind of animal which could be used for a sacrifice.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]** When they reached the place of sacrifice, Abraham tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an **altar**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** A priest would kill the animal and burn it on the **altar**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]** He (Gideon) built a new altar dedicated to God near where the **altar**  to the idol used to be and made a sacrifice to God on it.
