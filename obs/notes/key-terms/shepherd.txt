====== shepherd: berger F ======

===== Definition: =====

A shepherd is a person who takes care of sheep.

Un berger est une personne qui prend soin des moutons.

  * Sometimes a shepherd is the owner of the sheep, and sometimes he works for the owner.
  * Parfois un berger est le propriétaire des moutons, et parfois il travaille pour le propriétaire.
  * Shepherds lead sheep to grass so they can eat and to safe water so they can drink.
  * Les bergers emmènent les moutons manger dans des paturâges et vers de l'eau potable pour qu'ils puissent boire
  * They guide sheep so they don't get lost or wander into danger.
  * Ils dirigent les moutons, pour qu'ils ne se perdent pas ou ne s'aventurent pas vers des dangers.
  * They protect sheep from wild animals that try to attack them.
  * Ils protègent les moutons contre les animaux sauvages qui essayent de les attaquer
  * It is also used as a metaphor about leaders and those who follow them.
  * L'expression est aussi utilisée comme une métaphore pour des dirigeants et ceux qui les suivent

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:09-11|[09-11]]]** Moses became a **shepherd**  in the wilderness far away from Egypt.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a **shepherd**  from the town of Bethlehem. At different times while he was watching his father's sheep, David had killed both a lion and a bear that had attacked the sheep.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]** That night, there were some **shepherds**  in a nearby field guarding their flocks.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:23-08|[23-08]]]** The **shepherds**  soon arrived at the place where Jesus was and they found him lying in a feeding trough, just as the angel had told them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:30-03|[30-03]]]** To Jesus, these people were like sheep without a **shepherd**.
