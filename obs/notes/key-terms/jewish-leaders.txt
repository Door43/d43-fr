====== Jewish leaders Responsables juifs F ======

==== (Jewish leaders, religious leaders) ====

(Responsables juifs, Responsables religieux)

===== Facts: Les faits =====

The Jewish leaders were religious leaders, such as priests and experts in God's laws.

Les responsables juifs étaient des responsables religieux, tels que des prêtres et des docteurs de la loi de Dieu

  * Many of the religious leaders did not believe that Jesus was the Messiah and Son of God. They were jealous of Jesus and did not want other people to believe in him either.
  * De nombreux responsables religieux n'ont pas cru que Jésus était le Messie et le fils de Dieu. Ils étaient jaloux de Jésus et ne voulaient pas que d'autres personnes croient en Lui.
  * Some of the religious leaders did believe in Jesus–especially after he rose from the dead.
  * Certains des responsables religieux ont cru en Jésus- spécialement après sa résurrection d'entre les morts.
  * Many of the religious leaders believed that they were more righteous than other people, and they were too proud to admit their sins. They lied about Jesus to the Roman rulers and demanded that he be killed.
  * Beaucoup de responsables religieux croyaient qu'ils étaient plus justes que les autres, et étaient trop orgueilleux pour admettre leurs péchés. Ils ont dit des mensonges sur Jésus aux dirigeants romains et ont demandé à ce qu'il soit tué.
  * Jesus condemned the Jewish leaders for being hypocrites. They claimed to know God, but did not obey him.
  * Jésus a condamné les responsables juifs comme étant des hypocrites. Ils ont prétendu connaître Jésus, mais ne lui ont pas obéi.

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:24-03|[24-03]]]** Many **religious leaders**  also came to be baptized by John, but they did not repent or confess their sins.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:37-11|[37-11]]]** But the **religious leaders of the Jews**  were jealous, so they gathered together to plan how they could kill Jesus and Lazarus.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** He (Judas) knew that the **Jewish leaders**  denied that Jesus was the Messiah and that they were plotting to kill him.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:38-03|[38-03]]]** The **Jewish leaders**, led by the high priest, paid Judas thirty silver coins to betray Jesus.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:39-05|[39-05]]]** The **Jewish leaders**  all answered the high priest, "He (Jesus) deserves to die!"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:39-09|[39-09]]]** Early the next morning, the **Jewish leaders**  brought Jesus to Pilate, the Roman governor.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:39-11|[39-11]]]** But the **Jewish leaders**  and the crowd shouted, "Crucify him!"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:40-09|[40-09]]]** Then Joseph and Nicodemus, two **Jewish leaders**  who believed Jesus was the Messiah, asked Pilate for Jesus' body.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-07|[44-07]]]** The next day, the **Jewish leaders**  brought Peter and John to the high priest and the other **religious leaders**.
