====== covenant alliance F ======

===== Definition: Définition: =====

A covenant is a formal, binding agreement between two parties that they must fulfill.

<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>Une</font> <font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>alliance</font> est<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>un accord</font> formel<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>qui lie</font> deux<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>parties et</font> ils<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;></font> ont<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;></font> l'obligation<font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>de le</font> <font inherit line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.4;>respecter.</font>

  * This agreement can be between individuals, between groups of people, or between God and people
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Cette convention peut être contractée entre des individus, entre des goupes de personnes ou entre Dieu et son peuple.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When people make a covenant with each other, they say that they will do something, and they must do it.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Quand les gens font une alliance, ils disent qu'ils vont faire quelque chose et ils doivent le faire.</font>
  * Examples of covenants include marriage covenants, business agreements, and treaties between countries.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Les exemples d'alliances incluent les alliances de mariage, des contrats d'affaires, et les conventions entre les pays.</font>
  * On several occasions, God made a covenant with his people.
  * A plusieurs occasions, Dieu a fait une alliance avec son peuple.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In some of the covenants, God promised to fulfill his part without conditions.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Dans certaines alliances, Dieu a promis d'accomplir sa part sans conditions.</font>
  * In other covenants, God promised to fulfill his part if the people obeyed him and fulfilled their part of the covenant.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Dans d'autre alliances, Dieu a promis d'acomplir sa part de l'alliance si le people lui obéissait et respectait la sienne.</font>

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:04-09|[04-09]]]** Then God made a **covenant**  with Abram. A **covenant**  is an agreement between two parties.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]**"I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my **covenant**  will be with Isaac."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]** After a long time, Abraham died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the **covenant**  were passed on to Isaac.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The **covenant**  promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to Jacob."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-02|[13-02]]]** God said to Moses and the people of Israel, "If you will obey my voice and keep my **covenant**, you will be my prized possession, a kingdom of priests, and a holy nation."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-04|[13-04]]]** Then God gave them the **covenant**  and said, "I am Yahweh, your God, who saved you from slavery in Egypt. Do not worship other gods."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the **covenant**  that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai.
