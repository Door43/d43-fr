====== covenant; Aliance ======

===== Definition: =====

A covenant is a formal, binding agreement between two parties that they must fulfill.

<font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>l'alliance est est un accord entre deux partis.</font>

  * This agreement can be between individuals, between groups of people, or between God and people

         <font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>Cet agrement peut etre contracte entre des individus, entre un goupe de peuple ou entre Dieu et les gens.</font>

  * When people make a covenant with each other, they say that they will do something, and they must do it.

        <font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>Quand les gens contract une alliance entre eux et d'autres personnes, ils promettent de faire quelquechose et ils doivent le fair.</font>

  * Examples of covenants include marriage covenants, business agreements, and treaties between countries.

        <font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>les exemples d'alliances inclus l'alliance de mariage, l'alliance des affairs, et les traites entre les pays</font>

  * On several occasions, God made a covenant with his people.

Dans plusieurs occasions, Dieu a fait l'alliances avec son peuple

</font>

  * In some of the covenants, God promised to fulfill his part without conditions.

<font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>Dans certaines alliances, Dieu a promis d'accomplis sa part sans conditions.</font>

  * In other covenants, God promised to fulfill his part if the people obeyed him and fulfilled their part of the covenant.

        <font inherit/inherit;;#0000FF;;inherit>Dans d'autre alliances, Dieu a promit d'acomplis sa part si le people lui obei et respect leur part de l'alliance.</font>
