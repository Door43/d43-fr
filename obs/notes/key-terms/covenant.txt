====== covenant; Alliance ======

===== Definition: Définition: =====

A covenant is a formal, binding agreement between two parties that they must fulfill.

<font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Une alliance est un accord formel qui lie deux parties et qu'ils doivent le respecter.</font>

  * This agreement can be between individuals, between groups of people, or between God and people
  * <font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Cette convention peut être contractée entre des individus, entre un goupe de personne ou entre Dieu et son peuple.</font>

  * When people make a covenant with each other, they say that they will do something, and they must do it.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Quand les gens contract une alliance entre eux , qui les oblige à faire quelque chose de faire et ils doivent obigatoirement le faire.</font>

  * Examples of covenants include marriage covenants, business agreements, and treaties between countries.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Les exemples d'alliances inclus l'alliance de mariage, l'alliance des affaires, et les traités qui lient les pays.</font>

  * On several occasions, God made a covenant with his people.

Dans plusieurs occasions, Dieu a fait  une alliance avec son peuple.

</font>

  * In some of the covenants, God promised to fulfill his part without conditions.

<font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Dans certaines alliances, Dieu a promis d'accomplir sa part sans conditions</font>

  * In other covenants, God promised to fulfill his part if the people obeyed him and fulfilled their part of the covenant.

<font inherit/inherit;;rgb(0, 0, 255);;inherit>Dans d'autre alliances, Dieu a promit d'acomplis sa part si le people lui obei et respecte leur part de l'alliance.</font>
