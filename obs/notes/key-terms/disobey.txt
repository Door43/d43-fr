====== disobey  désobéir ======

===== Definition: =====

There are several ways to disobey someone. ** **

**Il y a plusieurs moyens à désobéir à quelqu'un.**

   * It is possible to disobey by refusing or failing to do something that was commanded.
  * **Il est possible de désobéir en refusant ou en échouant de faire quelque chose qui a été commandée.**
  * We can also disobey by doing something that was prohibited.
  * **Nous pouvons déobéir aussi en faisant quelque chose qui a été interdite.**
  * And we can disobey by doing something differently than we were instructed.
  * **Nous pouvons aussi désobéir en faisant quelque chose en façon différente de celle que nous avons été dirigés.**
To "disobey" is the opposite of to "obey."

A désobéir est le contraire d'obéir.

[Voir aussi: mal, péché, obéir]

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:02-11|[02-11]]]** God said to the man, "You listened to your wife and **disobeyed**  me."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** If the people obeyed these laws, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they **disobeyed**  them, God would punish them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-02|[16-02]]]** Because the Israelites kept **disobeying**  God, he punished them by allowing their enemies to defeat them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:35-12|[35-12]]]**"The older son said to his father, 'All these years I have worked faithfully for you! I never **disobeyed**  you, and still you did not give me one small goat so I could celebrate with my friends.'"
