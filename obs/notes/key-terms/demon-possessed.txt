====== demon-possessed  possession par les esprits démoniaque ======

===== Definition:  Définition: =====

Une personne démoniac possède un démon ou un esprit d'un démon qui contrôle ce qu'il fait ou qu'il pense.

  * Souvent un démoniaque se blessera ou fera mal aux autres parce que le démon le force à faire ces choses.
  * Jésus healed demon-possessed people by forcing the demons to come out of them.
  * This could also be translated as, "demon-controlled" or, "controlled by a demon" or, "having a demon."

[See also: **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:demon|demon]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:26-09|[26-09]]]** Many people who had **demons in them**  were brought to Jesus.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:32-02|[32-02]]]** When they reached the other side of the lake, a **demon-possessed**  man came running up to Jesus.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:32-06|[32-06]]]** The man **with the demon**  cried out in a loud voice, "What do you want with me, Jesus, Son of the Most High God? Please do not torture me!"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:32-09|[32-09]]]** The people from the town came and saw the man who used to **have the demons**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:47-03|[47-03]]]** Every day as they (Paul and Silas) walked there, a slave girl **possessed by a demon**  followed them.
