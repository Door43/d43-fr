====== Passover: Pâques  F ======

===== Facts: Les faits =====

This was a celebration that God wanted the Israelites to do each year in order to remember how God "passed over" the houses of the Israelites and did not kill their sons when he killed the firstborn sons of every Egyptian family.

C'était une célébration annuelle que Dieu demandait aux enfants d'Israël en souvenir de comment Il est passé outre les maisons des Israélites en épargnant leurs fils pendant qu'Il massacrait les premiers nés de toutes  les familles égyptiennes.

  * The Passover Feast, or celebration, includes a special meal that reminds people of the meal they ate that night. The meal included a lamb without any defect that they killed, roasted, and shared, as well as bread made without yeast.
  * La fête de Paques ou la célébration inclut un repas spécial qui rappelle au peuple le repas qu'ils ont partagé cette nuit là. Le repas etait composé d'un agneau sans défaut, tué, roti et partagé avec du pain sans levain.
  * God told the Israelites to eat this meal every year in order to remember and celebrate how God "passed over" their houses and how he set them free from slavery in Egypt.
  * Dieu avait dit aux enfants d'Israël de manger ce repas chaque année en mémoire de la célébration de la manière dont Dieu est passé outre leurs maisons et les a libéré de l'esclavage en Egypte.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-14|[12-14]]]** God commanded the Israelites to remember his victory over the Egyptians and their deliverance from slavery by celebrating the **Passover**  every year.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** Every year, the Jews celebrated the **Passover**. This was a celebration of how God had saved their ancestors from slavery in Egypt many centuries earlier.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:38-04|[38-04]]]** Jesus celebrated the **Passover**  with his disciples.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-09|[48-09]]]** When God saw the blood, he passed over their houses and did not kill their firstborn sons. This event is called the **Passover**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-10|[48-10]]]** Jesus is our **Passover**  Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the **Passover**  celebration.
