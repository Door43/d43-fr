====== miracle: miracles ======

==== (miracle, wonder, sign) ; miracle, merveille, des signes ====

===== Definition: =====

Miracles are things that could never happen unless God caused them. For example, it was a miracle when God brought a dead person back to life.

Les miracles sont des choses qui ne peuvent jamais arrivees avant que Dieu  ne puisent les causer .Par example C,etait un miracle de Dieu ramenai un mort a la vie

  * Miracles are sometimes called "wonders" because people are amazed by them. They are also called "signs" because they show that God is working through them.
  * Les miracles sont parfois appele merveille parce que les gens  par LUI
  * Since miracles are done by the power of God, they are sometimes called, "works of power."
  * depuis les miracles sont produits  par  la puissance de Dieu ils sont parfois appeles ( le travail de puissance de Dieu )
  * People do not have the power to do miracles on their own. However, God has given different people the power to do miracles on many occasions to accomplish his purposes.
  * les gens ne peuvent pas avoir la puissance de faire des miracles eux-memes. Cependant Dieu a donne aux differents le pouvoir de faire des miracles dans le but d,accomplir ses objectifs
  * For example, the prophets asked God to heal people, to provide food from nothing, and even to raise people from the dead, and God did those things.Par exemple, les prophetes ont demande a Dieu de guerir les gens, de pouvoir a la nourriture des gens qui n<ont  rien et de ressuciter des gens de la mort et Dieu fait ses choses
