====== miracle: miracles F ======

==== (miracle, wonder, sign) <font 14.166666030883789px line-height: 1.2;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>(miracle,</font> merveille<font 14.166666030883789px line-height: 1.2;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>,</font> signe<font 14.166666030883789px line-height: 1.2;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>)</font>  ====

===== Definition: <font 14.166666030883789px line-height: 1.2/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Définition</font>    <font 14.166666030883789px line-height: 1.2/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>:</font>     =====

Miracles are things that could never happen unless God caused them. For example, it was a miracle when God brought a dead person back to life.

Les miracles sont des choses qui ne peuvent jamais arriver à moins que Dieu les autorise. Par exemple, un miracle s'est produit quand Dieu a ramené une personne morte à la vie.

  * Miracles are sometimes called "wonders" because people are amazed by them. They are also called "signs" because they show that God is working through them.
  * Les miracles sont parfois appelés des "merveilles" parce que les gens ils les émerveillent. Ils sont aussi appelées des "signes" parce que l'oeuvre de Dieu se manifeste à travers eux.
  * Since miracles are done by the power of God, they are sometimes called, "works of power."
  * Etant donné que les miracles sont produits par la puissance de Dieu ils sont parfois appelés ( la puissance de Dieu )
  * People do not have the power to do miracles on their own. However, God has given different people the power to do miracles on many occasions to accomplish his purposes.
  * Les gens n'ont pas le pouvoir de faire des miracles eux-mêmes. Cependant Dieu a donne à differentes personne le pouvoir de faire des miracles dans le but d'accomplir ses objectifs
  * For example, the prophets asked God to heal people, to provide food from nothing, and even to raise people from the dead, and God did those things.
  * Par exemple, les prophètes ont demandé à Dieu le pouvoir de guèrir les gens, de pourvoir de la nourriture à des gens, et même de ressusciter les gens d'entre les morts et Dieu a fait ces choses

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]** Gideon asked God for two **signs**  so he could be sure that God would use him to save Israel.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:19-14|[19-14]]]** God did many **miracles**  through Elisha.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** Many of the Jews believed in Jesus because of this **miracle**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:43-06|[43-06]]]**"Men of Israel, Jesus was a man who did many mighty **signs**  and **wonders**  by the power of God, as you have seen and already know."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-02|[49-02]]]**] Jesus did many **miracles**  that prove he is God. He walked on water, calmed storms, healed many sick people, drove out demons, raised the dead to life, and turned five loaves of bread and two small fish into enough food for over 5,000 people.
