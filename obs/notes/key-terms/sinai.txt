====== Sinai: Sinai ======

==== (Sinai, Mount Sinai) :Sinai, Mount Sinai ====

===== Facts: =====

Sinai is the name of a mountain that was probably located in the southern part of what is now called the Sinai Peninsula.

**Sinai est le nom d'une ****montaigne**** qui etait probablement situee dans la partie sud de ce qui est maintenant appele : Sinai Peninsula**
   * The Israelites came to Mount Sinai as they were traveling from Egypt to the Promised Land.
  * **Les Israelite  venaient  au  Mont Sinai  pendant qu'ils voyageaient de l'Egypte a la terre promise**
  * Mount Sinai is part of a large, rocky desert.
  * ** Le Mont Sinai est  une large partie du desert rocky**
  * God gave Moses the Ten Commandments on Mount Sinai.
  * **Dieu a donne a Moise  the dix Commandements  sur le Mont Sinai**
[See also: **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:promised-land|Promised Land]]**, **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:ten-commandments|Ten Commandments]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-01|[13-01]]]** After God led the Israelites through the Red Sea, he led them through the wilderness to a mountain called **Sinai**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-03|[13-03]]]** Three days later, after the people had prepared themselves spiritually, God came down on top of **Mount Sinai**  with thunder, lightning, smoke, and a loud trumpet blast.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-11|[13-11]]]** For many days, Moses was on top of **Mount Sinai**  talking with God.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at **Sinai**.
