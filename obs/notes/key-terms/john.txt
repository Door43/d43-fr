====== John (the apostle) Jean (l'apôtre) F ======

===== Facts: Les faits =====

John was one of Jesus' twelve apostles and one of Jesus' closest friends.

Jean était l'un des douze apôtres et l'un des amis proches de Jésus.

  * He was a different person than John the Baptist.
  * Jean le Baptiste et l'apôtre Jean étaient deux personnes différentes.
  * After Jesus went back to heaven, John (the apostle) taught people about Jesus.
  * Après que Jésus soit remonté au ciel, Jean (l'apôtre) a enseigné à propos de Jésus aux gens.
  * John also wrote two books and some letters about Jesus that are included in the Bible.
  * Jean a aussi écrit deux livres et quelques épitres sur Jésus qui sont inclus dans la Bible.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:36-01|[36-01]]]** One day, Jesus took three of his disciples, Peter, James, and **John**  with him. (The disciple named **John**  was not the same person who baptized Jesus.) They went up on a high mountain by themselves.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-01|[44-01]]]** One day, Peter and **John**  were going to the Temple. As they approached the Temple gate, they saw a crippled man who was begging for money.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-06|[44-06]]]** The leaders of the Temple were very upset by what Peter and **John**  were saying. So they arrested them and put them into prison.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-07|[44-07]]]** The next day, the Jewish leaders brought Peter and **John**  to the high priest and the other religious leaders. They asked Peter and **John**, "By what power did you heal this crippled man?"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-09|[44-09]]]** The leaders were shocked that Peter and **John**  spoke so boldly because they could see that these men were ordinary men who were uneducated. But then they remembered that these men had been with Jesus. After they threatened Peter and **John**, they let them go.
