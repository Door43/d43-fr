====== God's law  La loi de Dieu ======

===== Definition: Définition =====

God's law is all of the commands and instructions that he gave to his people Israel.

La loi de Dieu est tous les commandements et les instructions qu'Il a donnés à son peuple, Israël.

  * God gave his people many different commands and instructions when he spoke with Moses on Mount Sinai.
  * Dieu a donné à son peuple plusieurs différents commandements et instructions quand Il a parlé à Moïse sur le Mont Sinaï.
  * The Ten Commandments were part of God's law. They were a concise statement of some of the most important responsibilities of Israel towards God and each other.
  * Les Dix Commandements faisaient partie de la loi de Dieu. C'était une déclaration concise de quelques unes des responsabilités les plus importantes d'Israel envers Dieu et les autres.
  * These commands deal with everyday things like worship, food, health, family relationships, and war.
  * Ces commandements ont affaire avec les choses de la vie quotidienne comme l'adoration, la nourriture, la santé, les relations de famille, et la guerre.
  * The laws that God gave to Israel were an expression of the moral nature of God. They teach us about the holy character of God, as well as the consequences of disobeying him.
  * Les lois que Dieu a données à Israël étaient une expression de la nature morale de Dieu. Elles nous renseignent sur le caractère saint de Dieu, aussi bien que les conséquence de la désobéissance envers Lui.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other **laws**  and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these **laws**, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would punish them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** Anyone who disobeyed **God's law**  could bring an animal to the altar in front of the Tent of Meeting as a sacrifice to God.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai. The people promised to remain faithful to God and follow **his laws**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-01|[16-01]]]** After Joshua died, the Israelites disobeyed God and did not drive out the rest of the Canaanites or obey **God's laws**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write **his law**  on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** Jesus answered, "What is written in **God's law**?"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:28-01|[28-01]]]** Jesus said to him, "Why do you call me 'good?' There is only one who is good, and that is God. But if you want to have eternal life, obey **God's laws**."
