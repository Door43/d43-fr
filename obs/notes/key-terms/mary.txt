====== Mary : Marie  F ======

===== Facts: les faits =====

Mary was the mother of Jesus and the wife of Joseph.

Marie étail la mére de Jesus et la femme de Joseph

  * The Holy Spirit miraculously caused Mary to become pregnant while she was a virgin. The baby in her was the Son of God.
  * Par la puissance du Saint-Esprit, Marie est tombée enceinte alors qu'elle était encore vierge. Le bébé qui était en son sein était le fils de Dieu.
  * Joseph took Mary as his wife, but Mary remained a virgin until after the baby was born.
  * Joseph a pris Marie comme sa femme, mais Marie était restée vierge jusqu'à la naissance du bébé.
  * When the baby was born, Mary and Joseph named him Jesus.
  * Quand le Bébé ést né, Mary et Joseph l'ont appelé Jésus

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:22-04|[22-04]]]** When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel appeared to Elizabeth's relative, whose name was **Mary**. She was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named Joseph. The angel said, "You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him Jesus and he will be the Messiah."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]** The angel explained, "The Holy Spirit will come to you, and the power of God will overshadow you. So the baby will be holy, the Son of God." **Mary**  believed and accepted what the angel said.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:22-06|[22-06]]]** Soon after the angel spoke to **Mary**, she went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard **Mary's**  greeting, Elizabeth's baby jumped inside her.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:23-02|[23-02]]]** The angel said, "Joseph, do not be afraid to take **Mary**  as your wife. The baby in her body is from the Holy Spirit."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and **Mary**  had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-01|[49-01]]]** An angel told a virgin named **Mary**  that she would give birth to God's Son. So while she was still a virgin, she gave birth to a son and named him Jesus.
