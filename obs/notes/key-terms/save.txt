====== save    sauver/délivrer  ======

==== (save, salvation, saved) ====

==== (sauver, le salut, sauvé) ====

===== Definition: =====

===== Définition: =====

To save someone or something is to rescue that person or thing from something bad.

Sauver quelqu'un ou quelque chose, c'est de délivrer cette personne ou cette chose d'une mauvaise chose.

  * In a physical sense, people can be saved from harm, danger, or death.
  * Au sens physique, les gens peuvent être sauvé du dommage, du danger ou de la mort.
  * In a spiritual sense, if a person has been "saved," it means that through Jesus' death on the cross, God has forgiven him and rescued him from being punished for his sin.
  * Au sens spirituel, si une personne a étée "sauvée," ça veut dire que Dieu lui a absous et lui a délivré du châtiment
  * Only God can save people from being punished for their sin.

[See also: **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sin]]**, **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:punish|punish]]**, **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:cross|cross]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:09-08|[09-08]]]** Moses tried to **save**  his fellow Israelite.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to **save**  the firstborn son of anyone who believed in him.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** Moses told the Israelites, "Stop being afraid! God will fight for you today and **save**  you."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-13|[12-13]]]** The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to praise God because he **saved**  them from the Egyptian army.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** This pattern repeated many times: the Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to **save**  them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]**"You crucified Jesus, but God raised him to life again! You rejected him, but there is no other way to be **saved**  except through the power of Jesus!"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:47-11|[47-11]]]**] The jailer trembled as he came to Paul and Silas and asked, "What must I do to be **saved**?" Paul answered, "Believe in Jesus, the Master, and you and your family will be **saved**."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-12|[49-12]]]** Good works cannot **save**  you.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-13|[49-13]]]** God will **save**  everyone who believes in Jesus and receives him as their Master. But he will not **save**  anyone who does not believe in him.
