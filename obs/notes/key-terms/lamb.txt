====== lamb agneau  F ======

==== (lamb, Lamb of God) (agneau, agneau de Dieu) ====

===== Definition: Définition: =====

In the Bible, a lamb is a young sheep or goat. A sheep is a four-legged animal with a thick woolly hair. People raise them for their wool or as a source of food. Sheep are defenseless and timid. Goats are similar animals, and are used mainly for food.

Dans la Bible, un agneau est un jeune mouton. Un mouton est un animal à quatre pattes avec une épaisse fourrure en laine.Les hommes les élèvent pour la laine et principalement la viande. Les moutons sont sans défense et timides. Les chévres sont des animaux, et sont utilisées principalement pour la nourriture.

  * God's people used to sacrifice sheep and lambs to Him. The ones they sacrificed had to be physically perfect, with no defects.
  * Le peuple de Dieu avait l'habitude de sacrifier le mouton et l'agneau à Dieu. L'animal sacrifié devait être physiquement parfait, sans defaut.
  * Jesus is called the "Lamb of God" because he was morally perfect–completely without sin–and his sacrifice paid for people's sins.
  * Jésus est appelé "l'agneau de Dieu" parce quíl est moralement parfait-complètement sans péché et son sacrifice a payé pour les péchés des hommes.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:05-07|[05-07]]]** As Abraham and Isaac walked to the place of the sacrifice Isaac asked, "Father, we have wood for the sacrifice, but where is the **lamb**?"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to save the firstborn son of anyone who believed in him. Each family had to choose a perfect **lamb**  or goat and kill it.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:24-06|[24-06]]]** The next day, Jesus came to be baptized by John. When John saw him, he said, "Look! There is the **Lamb of God**  who will take away the sin of the world."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:45-08|[45-08]]]** He read, "They led him like a **lamb**  to be killed, and as a **lamb**  is silent, he did not say a word.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-08|[48-08]]]** When God told Abraham to offer his son, Isaac, as a sacrifice, God provided a **lamb**  for the sacrifice instead of his son, Isaac. We all deserve to die for our sins! But God provided Jesus, the **Lamb**  of God, as a sacrifice to die in our place.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-09|[48-09]]]** When God sent the last plague on Egypt, he told each Israelite family to kill a perfect **lamb**  and spread its blood around the tops and sides of their door frames.
