====== Isaiah Esaïe F ======

===== Facts: Les faits =====

Isaiah was a prophet of God.

Esaïe était un prophète de Dieu.

  * Isaiah wrote many prophecies about Israel that came true while he was still living.
  * Esaïe a écrit beaucoup de prophéties concernant Israël qui se sont accomplis pendant qu'il était encore vivant.
  * Isaiah is especially known for the prophecies he wrote about the Messiah that came true 700 years later when Jesus was living on earth.
  * Esaïe est particulièrement connu pour les prophéties qu'il a écrites concernant le Messie et qui se sont réalisées 700 ans après, quand Jésus vivait sur la terre.
  * Jesus and his disciples quoted Isaiah's prophecies to teach people about the Messiah.
  * Jésus et ses disciples ont cité les prophéties d'Esaïe pour enseigner aux gens à propos du Messie.
  * The Book of Isaiah is one of the major books of the Bible.
  * Le livre d'Esaïe est un des livres majeurs de la Bible.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-10|[21-10]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  said the Messiah would live in Galilee, comfort broken-hearted people, and proclaim freedom to captives and release to prisoners.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-11|[21-11]]]** The prophet **Isaiah**  also prophesied that the Messiah would be hated without reason and rejected.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-12|[21-12]]]****Isaiah**  prophesied that people would spit on, mock, and beat the Messiah.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** They handed him (Jesus) the scroll of the prophet **Isaiah**  so that he would read from it. Jesus opened up the scroll and read part of it to the people.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:45-08|[45-08]]]** When Philip approached the chariot, he heard the Ethiopian reading from what the prophet **Isaiah**  wrote.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:45-10|[45-10]]]** Philip explained to the Ethiopian that **Isaiah**  was writing about Jesus.
