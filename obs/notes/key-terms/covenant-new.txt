====== New Covenant Nouvelle Alliance  F ======

===== Definition: Définition =====

God made a covenant with Israel through his prophet Moses, and later promised that he would make a New Covenant.

Dieu a fait une alliance avec Israël par le biais de son prophète Moise, et plus tard il promit qu'il ferait une Nouvelle Alliance.

  * The New Covenant is better than the old one because it is based on the sacrifice of Jesus for the sins of everyone.
  * La Nouvelle Alliance est meilleure que l' ancienne parce que la Nouvelle Alliance est basée sur le sacrifice de Jésus pour le péché de tout le monde.
  * This New Covenant came into effect when God's Son died and came back to life.
  * Cette Nouvelle Alliance a pris effet lorsque le Fils de Dieu est mort et est ressuscité d'entre les morts.
  * Believers keep the New Covenant by trusting God to forgive them and save them because of the sacrificial death and resurrection of Jesus.
  * Les croyants gardent la Nouvelle Alliance en croyant que Dieu les pardonne et les sauve à cause du sacrifice de la mort et de la resurrection de Jésus.
  * God writes the New Covenant on the hearts of believers. As a result of this, Christians want to obey God, and they begin to live holy lives that demonstrate that God is working in them
  * Dieu écrit la Nouvelle Alliance dans les coeurs des croyants. Par conséquent, les Chrétiens décident d'obéir à Dieu, et ils commencent à avoir des vies sanctifiées qui démontre que Dieu vit en eux.
  * The New Covenant will be fulfilled when God establishes his reign on earth. Everything will once again be very good, as it was at creation. There will be no more sin, suffering, or injustice. Instead, all of God's people will dwell with him in peace, love, and harmony.
  * La Nouvelle Alliance se réalisera quand Dieu établira son reigne sur la terre.Tout sera une fois de plus très bon, comme c'était lors de la création. Il n'y aura plus de péché, de souffrance, ou d'injustice. A la place, tous les enfants de Dieu habiteront avec Dieu, dans la paix, l'amour, et l'harmonie

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** Through the prophet Jeremiah, God promised that he would make a **New Covenant**, but not like the covenant God made with Israel at Sinai. In the **New Covenant**, God would write his law on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins. The Messiah would start the **New Covenant**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-14|[21-14]]]** Through the Messiah's death and resurrection, God would accomplish his plan to save sinners and start the **New Covenant**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:38-05|[38-05]]]** Then Jesus took a cup and said, "Drink this. It is my blood of the **New Covenant**  that is poured out for the forgiveness of sins. Do this to remember me every time you drink it."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:48-11|[48-11]]]** But God has now made a **New Covenant**  that is available to everyone. Because of this **New Covenant**, anyone from any people group can become part of God's people by believing in Jesus.
