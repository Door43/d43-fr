====== glory : Gloire ======

===== Definition: =====

Something or someone has glory when it is extremely good in some way.

**Quelque chose ou quelqu,un est dans la gloire quand il est extrement bon  en d,autre terme**

   * Glory can refer to extreme greatness, power, or wealth.
  * **la gloire peut se referer a un extreme  pouvoir grandeur ou richesse**
  * Glory can also refer to the honor or reputation that someone has because of his extreme greatness.
  * La gloire peut a**ussi**** se  referer a  la reputation que quelqu,un a  a cause son estreme grandeur**
  * God possesses all glory. It is the outshining of his beauty, holiness, honor, and power.
  * **Dieu dispose de toute la Gloire.il est  ble reflet de sa beaute de sa saintete et de son pouvoir**
  * To give God glory is to honor, praise, and worship him.
  * **Donner Gloire a Dieu revient a l,honorer,a le louer et l,adorer**
  * When we say that something brings God glory, it means that it leads people to honor, praise, and worship him.
  * Quand nous disons que quelque chose apporte la gloire a Dieu. cela signifie qu,Il dirige le peuple, l,honore , louer et l.adorer
