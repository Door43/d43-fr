====== repent repentir ======

==== (repent, repentance) ====

(repentir, repentance)

===== Definition: Définition: =====

To repent is to regret or be sorrowful for something and to decide to stop doing it.

Se repentir c'est de regretter ou être triste à propos de quelque chose et de décider d'arrêter.

  * To repent from sin means to admit to God that it is wrong, to stop doing that sin, and to begin to obey God.
  * Repentir de ses péchés signifie  admettre devant Dieu ce qui est mauvais, abandonner ce péché, et commencer à suivre Dieu.
  * Repentance is the act of repenting.
  * La repentance est l'acte de repentir.
  * When people truly repent of their sins, God forgives them.
  * Quand les gens se rependent vraiment de leurs péchés, Dieu les pardonne.
  * The term, "repent" can be translated as, "turn from your sins and be sorry for them."
  * Le terme, "repentir" peut être traduit comme "quitter ses péchés et les regretter".

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-02|[16-02]]]** After many years of disobeying God and being oppressed by their enemies, the Israelites **repented**  and asked God to rescue them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** David **repented**  of his sin and God forgave him.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:19-18|[19-18]]]** They (prophets) warned people that God would destroy them if they did not **repent**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:24-02|[24-02]]]** Many people came out to the wilderness to listen to John. He preached to them, saying, "**Repent**, for the kingdom of God is near!"
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]**""It was also written in the scriptures that my disciples will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to **receive**  forgiveness for their sins. "
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-05|[44-05]]]**"So now, **repent**  and turn to God so that your sins will be washed away."
