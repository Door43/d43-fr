====== Nazareth Nazareth ======

===== Facts: Faits: =====

Nazareth is a town in the region of Galilee in northern Israel.

Nazareth est une ville dans la région de Galilée au nord d'Israel.

  * Joseph and Mary were from Nazareth, and this is where they raised Jesus.
  * Joseph et Marie étaient de Nazareth, et c'est là que Jésus a grandi.
  * Many of the people in Nazareth did not respect the teaching of Jesus, because Jesus had grown up among them, and they thought that he was just an ordinary person.
  * Beaucoup de gens de Nazareth n'ont pas respecté l'enseignement de Jésus, parce que Jésus a grandi parmi eux, et ils pensaient qu'il était une personne ordinaire.
  * The people of Nazareth tried to kill Jesus when he claimed to be the Messiah.
  * Les gens de Nazareth ont tenté de tuer Jésus quand il a déclaré être le Messie.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in **Nazareth**  to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]** Jesus went to the town of **Nazareth**  where he had lived during his childhood.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:26-07|[26-07]]]** The people of **Nazareth**  dragged Jesus out of the place of worship and brought him to the edge of a cliff to throw him off of it in order to kill him.
