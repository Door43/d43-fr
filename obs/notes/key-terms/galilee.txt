====== Galilee - Galilée ======

===== Facts: faits =====

Galilée is the most northern part of Israel, farther north than Samaria.

La Galilée est la région la plus au Nord, plus au Nord que la Samarie

  * Galilee is bordered on the east by a large lake called "the Sea of Galilee."
  * La Galilée est bordée à l'Est par un grand lac appelé " la mer de Galilée.
  * Jesus grew up and lived in the town of Nazareth in Galilee.
  * Jésus a grandi et a vécu dans la ville de Nazareth en Galilée.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:21-10|[21-10]]]** The prophet Isaiah said the Messiah would live in **Galilee**, comfort broken-hearted people, and proclaim freedom to captives and release to prisoners.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:26-01|[26-01]]]** After overcoming Satan's temptations, Jesus returned in the power of the Holy Spirit to the region of **Galilee**  where he lived.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:39-06|[39-06]]]** Finally, the people said, "We know that you were with Jesus because you both are from **Galilee**."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:41-06|[41-06]]]** Then the angel told the women, "Go and tell the disciples, 'Jesus has risen from the dead and he will go to **Galilee**  ahead of you.'"
