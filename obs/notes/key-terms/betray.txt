====== betray: trahïr   F ======

==== (to betray, betrayer, betrayed): ====

==== (trahir, traite, trahi) ====

===== Definition: =====

To betray someone is to gain that person's trust, but then to act in a way that deceives that person or is harmful to him in some way.

Trahir quelqu'un c'est gagner <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>la</font>    confiance <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>de</font>    <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>cette</font>    personne<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>,</font>    mais ensuite agir d'une manière quii la déçoit ou <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>la</font>    blesse <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>en</font>    quelque sorte<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>.</font>

  * Judas was "the betrayer" because he told the Jewish leaders how to capture Jesus.
  * Judas a été le traitre parce qu"il a dit au chef des Juifs comment arrêter Jésus.
  * The betrayal by Judas was especially evil because Judas was an apostle of Jesus, because Judas betrayed Jesus for money, and because the betrayal ended with the unjust execution of Jesus.
  * La trahison de Judas a été spécialement diabolique parce que Judas était un disciple de Jésus, parce que Judas a trahi Jésus pour de l'argent, et parce que la trahison a fini par l'exécution injuste de Jésus.
