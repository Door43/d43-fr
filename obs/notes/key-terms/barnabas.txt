====== Barnabas : Barnabas ======

===== Facts: les Faits =====

Barnabas was was one of the early Christians who lived during the time of the apostles.

**Barnabas a ete un des premiers  Chretiens qui a vecu pendant le temps des apotres**

   * When Saul (Paul) became a Christian, Barnabas encouraged the other believers to accept him as a fellow believer.
  * **Quand Saul ( Paul ) ete devenu chretien,Barnabas a encourage les autres chretiens a l'accepter comme croyant**
  *
  * Barnabas and Paul traveled together to preach the good news about Jesus in different cities.
  * **Barnabas et Paul ont voyage ensemble pour precher la bonne nouvelle de la foi en Jesus  dans diffrentes villes**
