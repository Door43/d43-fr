====== proud : orgueilleux ======

===== Definition: =====

A proud person thinks of himself as better than others, or as more important than others, and often does not admit his faults.

**Un ( Une ) orgueilleux ( euse ) pense etre le meilleur de tous  ou comme le plus important  de tous, et il n'admet pas ses fautes le plus souvent**

   * Other English words that mean the same as proud are "arrogant," "conceited," and "self-important".
  * certains mots anglais  ont les memes tels que fier, orgueilleux, arrogant, vante et auto- suffisant ( auto - centrique )
  * "They were proud" could be translated as "They thought highly of themselves."
  * Ils sont orgueilleux peut se traduire aussi comme : Il ont des hautes pensees ( opinion ) d'eux meme

Being proud can lead a person to disobey God.

**Un etre orgeuilleux peut amener des gens a desobeir  a Dieu**

The opposite of "proud" is "humble."

[See also: **[[:fr:obs:notes:key-terms:humble|humble]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:04-02|[04-02]]]** They were very **proud**, and they did not care about what God said.

But he did not like the prayer of the religious leader. God will humble everyone who is **proud**, and he will lift up whoever humbles himself."
