====== Jacob  Jacob ======

===== Facts: Les faits =====

Jacob was the second of the twin sons that were born to Isaac and Rebekah. The first twin born was Esau.

Jacob etait le second des jumeaux qui etaient nés d'Isaac et de Rebecca. Le premier né était Esau.

  * Jacob's name means "deceiver" or "trickster."
  * Le nom de Jacob signifie "
  * Jacob was clever and deceptive. He found ways to take both the firstborn inheritance rights and the blessing from his older brother, Esau.
  * Jacob etait intelligent et. Il a trouvé des moyens de s'emparer de l'heritaeg du fils ainé et des bénedictions de son frere ainé.
  * Esau was angry and planned to kill him, so Jacob left his homeland. But he later returned and lived peacefully with his brother.
  * Esau etait faché et planifiait de le tuer, alors Jacob a quitté sa terre natale. Mais il est revenu plus tard et a vécu en paix avec son frère.
  * God changes Jacob's name to Israel, which means, "He struggles with God."
  * Dieu a changé le nom de Jacob en Israël, ce qui signifie, "Il a lutté avec Dieu."
  * God kept his covenant to Abraham and his descendants through Abraham's son, Isaac, and Isaac's son, Jacob.
  * Dieu a gardé son alliance avec Abraham et ses descendants par le biais du fils d'Abraham, Isaac, et le fils d'Isaac, Jacob.
  * Jacob had twelve sons. Their descendants became the twelve tribes of Israel.
  * Jacob a eu douze fils. Leurs descendants sont devenus les douze tribus d'Israël.
