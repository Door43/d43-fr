====== Bathsheba: Bath-Scheba ======

===== Facts: Les Faits =====

Bathsheba was the wife of Uriah, a soldier in King David's army. She became the wife of David, and the mother of Solomon.

**Bath-Scheba a ete la femme d Uriah, un soldat dans l'armée de David. Elle était devenue la femme de David, et la mère de Salomon**

   * David committed adultery with Bathsheba while she was married to Urie.
  * **David a commis  l'adultère avec Bath-Scheba alors qu'elle était mariée à Urie**
  *
  * When Bathsheba became pregnant with David's child, David caused Uriah to be killed in battle. David then married Bathsheba.
  * **Quand Bath-Scheba devenait enceinte avec David, David avait ordodonné  à ce que Urie soit tué au combat**
  *
  * God punished David for his sin by causing the child to die several days after he was born.
  *  **Dieu a puni David pour son péché ayant causé la mort d'un enfant avant qu'il soit né** .
  *

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-10|[17-10]]]** One day, when all of David's soldiers were away from home fighting battles, he got up from an afternoon nap and saw a beautiful woman bathing. Her name was **Bathsheba**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-11|[17-11]]]** A short time later **Bathsheba**  sent a message to David saying that she was pregnant.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-12|[17-12]]]****Bathsheba's**  husband, a man named Uriah, was one of David's best soldiers.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** After Uriah was killed, David married **Bathsheba**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-14|[17-14]]]** Later, David and **Bathsheba**  had another son, and they named him Solomon.
