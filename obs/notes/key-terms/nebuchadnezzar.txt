====== Nebuchadnezzar Nebucadnetsar ======

===== Facts: Faits: =====

Nebuchadnezzar was a king of the Babylonian Empire. This was a powerful empire during the time of the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah.

Nebucadnetsar a été un roi de l'empire de Babylonien. C'était un puissant empire de la même période que le royaume d'Israel et le royaume de Juda.

  * Nebuchadnezzar had a powerful army that conquered many other people groups.
  * Nebucadnetsar avait une armée puissante qui envahissait les autres peuples.
  * Under Nebuchadnezzar's leadership, the Babylonian army attacked and conquered the kingdom of Judah, and took most of the people of Judah to Babylon as captives. This "Babylonian Exile" lasted 70 years.
      * Sous le reigne de Nebucadnetsar, l'armée a attaqué et conquit le royaume de Juda, et a ramené la plupart du peuple de Juda à Babylone en captivité. Cet "exil des Babyloniens" a duré 70 ans.

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** About 100 years after the Assyrians destroyed the kingdom of Israel, God sent **Nebuchadnezzar**, king of the Babylonians, to attack the kingdom of Judah.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** The king of Judah agreed to be **Nebuchadnezzar's**  servant and pay him a lot of money every year.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:20-08|[20-08]]]** To punish the king of Judah for rebelling, **Nebuchadnezzar's**  soldiers killed the king's sons in front of him and then made him blind.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]****Nebuchadnezzar**  and his army took almost all of the people of the kingdom of Judah to Babylon, leaving only the poorest people behind to plant the fields.
