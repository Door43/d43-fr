====== Gideon: Gedeon ======

===== Facts: les faits =====

Gideon was an Israelite man whom God raised up to deliver Israel from their enemies.

**Gedeon est un Israliste que Dieu a suscite pour delivrer Israel de ses ennemis**

   * Gideon lived during the time, many years after the Israelites moved into Canaan, when a group of people called the Midianites kept attacking them.
  * **Gedeon vivait durant des annees apres  que les Israelites se soint deplacer en Canaan , quand un groupe de gens appele Medianites **les attaquaient** **
  * Gideon was very fearful of the Midianites, but God used him to lead the Israelites to fight against the Midianites and defeat them.
  * **Gedeon avait tres peur des Medianites, mais Dieu l,avait utilise pour diriger les Israelites a combattre les Medianites et ainsi de les ecraser.**
===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-05|[16-05]]]** The angel of Yahweh came to **Gideon**  and said, "God is with you, mighty warrior. Go and save Israel from the Midianites."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-06|[16-06]]]****Gideon's**  father had an altar dedicated to an idol. God told **Gideon**  to tear down that altar.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]** There were so many of them (Midianites) that they could not be counted. **Gideon**  called the Israelites together to fight them.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]****Gideon**  called the Israelites together to fight them. **Gideon**  asked God for two signs so he could be sure that God would use him to save Israel.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-10|[16-10]]]** 32,000 Israelite soldiers came to **Gideon**, but God told him this was too many.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-12|[16-12]]]** Then **Gideon**  returned to his soldiers and gave each of them a horn, a clay pot, and a torch.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-15|[16-15]]]** The people wanted to make **Gideon**  their king.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-16|[16-16]]]** Then **Gideon**  used the gold to make a special garment like the high priest used to wear. But the people started worshiping it as if it were an idol.
