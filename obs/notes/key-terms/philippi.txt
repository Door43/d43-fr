====== Philippi: Philippes ======

===== Facts: =====

Philippi was a city located in the northern part of ancient Greece.

**philipppes a ete  une ville localisee dans la paritie nord de l'ancienne Grece**

   * Paul and Silas traveled to Philippi to preach about Jesus to the people there.
  * **paul et Silasvenaient a Philippes pour precher la parole de Jesus au peuple qui s'y trouvaient**
  * Paul and Silas were arrested in Philippi, but God miraculously freed them.
  * **Paul et Silas avaient ete arrete a Philippes . Mais Dieu les a miraculeusement libere**
  * Paul wrote the letter to the Philippians in the Bible to the Christians in Philippi.
  *  **Dans la Bible, Paul ecrivait la lettre aux philippiens :  aux chretiens de Philippes **

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:47-01|[47-01]]]** One day, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of **Philippi**  to proclaim the good news about Jesus.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:47-13|[47-13]]]** The next day the leaders of the city released Paul and Silas from prison and asked them to leave **Philippi**.
