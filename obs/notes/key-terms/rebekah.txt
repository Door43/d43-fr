====== Rebekah Rebecca ======

===== Facts: Faits: =====

Rebekah was a daughter of one of Abraham's nephews. Her grandfather was Abraham's brother.

Rebecca était la fille d'un des neveux d'Abraham. Son grand père était le frère d'Abraham.

  * God chose Rebekah to be the wife of Abraham's son Isaac.
  * Dieu a choisi Rebecca pour qu'elle soit la femme d'Issac le fils d'Abraham.
  * Rebekah left her country and went with Abraham's servant to go to Isaac's country, marry Isaac, and live there with his family.
  * Rebecca a quitté son pays et est partie avec le serviteur d'Abraham pour aller dans le pays d'Isaac, et y vivre avec la famille d'Isaac.
  * For a long time Rebekah did not have any children, but finally God blessed her with twin boys, Esau and Jacob.
  * Rebecca a mis un longtemps avant d'avoir des enfants, finallement Dieu l'a béni avec des garcons jumeaux, Esau et Jacob.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:06-02|[06-02]]]** After a very long journey to the land where Abraham's relatives lived, God led the servant to **Rebekah**. She was the granddaughter of Abraham's brother.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:06-06|[06-06]]]** God told **Rebekah**, "There are two nations inside of you."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, **Rebekah**  loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:07-03|[07-03]]]** Isaac wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, **Rebekah**  and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:07-06|[07-06]]]** But **Rebekah**  heard of Esau's plan. So she sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives.
