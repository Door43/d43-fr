====== Promised Land :La terre promise ======

==== (Promised Land, Canaan) : terre promise, Canaan ====

===== Facts: les faits =====

This was the land that God had promised to give to Abraham and his descendants.

**C'etait la terre que Dieu a promise  de donner a Abraham  et ses descendants**

   * After Abraham's descendants had lived in Canaan for many years, there was a famine and they had to move to the country of Egypt. Over 400 years later, God brought them back to Canaan.
  *  **Apres que  les descendants d'Abraham avainet vecu au Canaan  pendant plusieurs annees, Il y avait une famine et ils avainet demenage au pays d'Egypte pendant plus de 400 ans.Dieu les a ramene au Canaan**
  * Canaan was also called the "Promised Land" by Abraham's descendants.
  * ** Canaaan a te aussi appele la Terre promises par les decendants d<Abraham**
  * Because of Israel's disobedience, God took the people out of Canaan for seventy years during the Exile. Then, he brought them back to the Promised land again.
  * ** Parce qu' Israel a desobei,  Dieu les a eloignes de Canaan pendant 70 ans durant le temps d'exil. En suite iI les a ramenesa nouveau  a la terre promise **

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-01|[12-01]]]** They (Israelites) were no longer slaves, and they were going to the **Promised Land**!
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:14-01|[14-01]]]** After God had told the Israelites the laws he wanted them to obey as part of his covenant with them, God began leading them from Mount Sinai toward the **Promised Land**, which was also called Canaan.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:14-02|[14-02]]]** God had promised Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob that he would give the **Promised Land**  to their descendants, but now there were many people groups living there.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:14-14|[14-14]]]** Then God led the people to the edge of the **Promised Land**  again.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-02|[15-02]]]** The Israelites had to cross the Jordan River to enter into the **Promised Land**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:15-12|[15-12]]]** After this battle, God gave each tribe of Israel its own section of the **Promised Land**.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** This period of time when God's people were forced to leave the **Promised Land**  is called the Exile.
