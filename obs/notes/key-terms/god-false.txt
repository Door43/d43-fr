====== god: dieu ======

==== (god, gods) dieu dieux ====

===== Definition: =====

A god is something that people worship because they believe it has great power to affect their lives

**un dieu est quelque chose. que le peuple adore parce qu'ils croyaient qu"il avait un grand  pouvoir qui affecterait leurs vies**
