====== Deliverer Libérateur F ======

**<font 16px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>​Definition définition</font>    **
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The word "deliverer" can also be translated as, "rescuer."</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Le mot "libérateur" peut aussi se traduire par "sauveur."</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The deliverers in Israel were leaders who rescued the Israelite people from their enemies by leading them in battle against their enemies.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Les libérateurs en Israël étaient des dirigeants qui ont délivré le peuple de ses ennemis en prenant les commandes des batailles contre ses ennemis.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In many cases, the deliverer functioned as a military leader.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Dans la plupart des cas, le libérateur a joué le rôle de dirigeant militaire.</font>

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** ===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====  **</font>

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>[16-16]]]  ** They (Israel) finally asked God for help again, and God sent them another  ** deliverer</font>    **. **

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>[16-17]]]  ** Over many years, God sent many  ** deliverers** who saved the Israelites from their enemies.</font>
