====== disciple(disciple) ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The term "disciple" refers to a person who follows a teacher and learns from him.

Le terme "disciple' se réfère à quelqu'un qui suit et apprend d'un enseignant.

  * The people who followed Jesus around, listening to his teachings and obeying them, were called his "disciples." John the Baptist also had disciples.
  * Les gens qui suivaient Jésus,  écoutaient et obéissaient ses enseignements s'appelaient ses "disciples". Jean- Baptiste avait aussi des disciples.
  * At different times during Jesus' ministry, there were many disciples who followed him and heard his teachings.
  * A divers moments durant le ministère de Jésus, il y eut beaucoup de disciples qui l'ont suivi et ont entendu ses enseignements.
  * From among the many disciples who followed Jesus, he chose twelve to be his closest followers; these men became known as his "apostles." Jesus' twelve apostles continued to also be called "disciples."
  * Parmi les nombreux disciples qui l'ont suivi, Jésus en a choisi 12 pour être ses plus proches; ces hommes ont été connus en tant que ses "apôtres." On a aussi continué à appeler les 12 apôtres de Jésus "ses disciples."
  * Just before Jesus went up to heaven, he commanded his disciples to teach other people about him so that those people could become his disciples, too.
  * Avant d'aller au ciel, Jésus a commandé à ses disciples d'enseigner les autres gens à son sujet, afin qu'eux aussi deviennent ses disciples.
  * In modern times as well, anyone who believes in Jesus and obeys his teachings is also called a disciple of Jesus.
  * De nos jours aussi,on appelle un disciple, quiconque croit en Jésus et obéit à son enseignement.

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestions de traduction: =====

  * The term "disciple" could be translated by a word or phrase that means "follower" or "student" or "pupil" or "learner."
  * On pourrait traduire le terme "disciple" par un mot ou une expression tel que "celui qui suit" ou "étudiant" ou "élève" ou "apprenti."
  * Make sure that the translation of this term does not refer only to a student who learns in a classroom.
  * Soyez sûr que la traduction de ce terme ne se réfère pas seulement à un étudiant qui apprend dans une classe.
  * The translation of this term should be different from the translation of "apostle."
  * La traduction de ce terme devrait être différente de la traduction du mot "apôtre."
