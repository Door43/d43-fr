====== believe, believe in, belief(croire,croire en,croyance) ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The terms "believe" and "believe in" are closely related, but have slightly different meanings:

Les termes "croire" et "croire en" sont similaires mais leur définitions sont un peu différentes:

=== 1. believe croire ===

  * To believe something is to accept or trust that it is true.
  * Croire quelquechose c'est en accepter ou avoir confiance en sa réalité.
  * To believe someone is to acknowledge that what that person has said is true.
  * Croire quelqu'un est reconnaitre la vérité de ses paroles.

=== 2. believe in / croire en quelqu'un ===

  * To believe in someone means to trust in that person. It means to trust that the person is who he says he is, that he always speaks the truth, and that he will do what he has promised to do.
  * Croire en quelqu'un signifie mettre sa confiance en cette personne. Cela signifie croire en son identité, à la vérité de ses paroles, à l'exécution de ses promesses.
  * When a person truly believes in something, he will act in such a way that shows that belief.
  * Lorsqu'une personne croit vraiment en quelquechose, ses actions démontrent sa croyance.
  * The phrase "have faith in" usually has the same meaning as "believe in."
  * La phrase "avooir foi en" a normalement le même sens que "croire en."
  * To "believe in Jesus" means to believe that he is the Son of God, that he is God himself who also became human and who died as a sacrifice to pay for our sins. It means to trust him as Savior and live in a way that honors him.
  * "Croire en Jésus" signifie, croire qu'Il est le Fils de Dieu, qu'Il est Dieu lui-même incarné, et qu'Il est mort en tant que sacrifice pour payer la dette de nos péchés. Cela signifie mettre sa confiance en Lui en  tant que Sauveuret vivre d'une manière qui l'honore.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * To "believe" could be translated as to "know to be true" or "know to be right."
  * To "believe in" could be translated as, "trust completely" or "rely on and know to be true."
