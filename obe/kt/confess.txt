====== confess/confesser ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

To confess means to admit or assert that something is true. This can refer to admitting that we have sinned and desiring to stop that sin. It also can refer to boldly stating the truth about God.

Confesser signifie admettre ou affirmer que quelquechose est véridique. Cela peut être admettre que nous avons péché et désirer d'arrêter. Cela peut aussi signifier déclarer avec force la vérité concernant Dieu.

  * The Bible says that if people confess their sins to God, he will forgive them.
  * La Bible dit que si les gens confessent leurs péchés à Dieu, Il les pardonnera.
  * James the apostle wrote in his letter that when believers confess their sins to each other, this brings spiritual healing.
  * Jacques, l'apôtre écrivit dans sa lettre que lorsque les croyants confessent leurs péchés entre eux, une guérison spirituelle intervient.
  * The apostle Paul wrote to the Philippians that someday everyone will confess that Jesus is Lord.
  * L'apôtre Paul écrivit aux Philippiens que tout homme confessera que Jésus est Seigneur.
  * Paul also said that if people confess that Jesus is Lord and believe that God raised him from the dead, they will be saved.
  * Paul déclare aussi que si les gens confessent que Jésus est Seigneur et croient que Dieu l'a résussisté des morts, ils seront sauvés.

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestions de traduction: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this term could include, "profess," "admit," "testify," "declare," "acknowledge," or "affirm."
  * Selon le contexte, on pourrait traduire ce terme différemment tel que " professer", "admettre", témoigner", "déclarer", "reconnaitre",ou "affirmer."
