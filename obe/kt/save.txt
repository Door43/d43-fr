====== save(sauver) ======

===== Definition: Définition: =====

To save someone or something is to rescue that person or thing from something bad or harmful.


Sauver quelqu'un ou quelquechose c'est les délivrer de quelquechose de mauvais ou dangereux.  

  * In a physical sense, people can be saved from harm, danger, or death.
  * Physiquement, on peut sauver des gens d'un péril, d'un danger, ou de la mort.
  * In a spiritual sense, if a person has been "saved," it means that through Jesus' death on the cross, God has forgiven him and rescued him from being punished for his sin.
  * Au sens spirituel, si un personne a été "sauvé" cela signifie que par la mort de Jésus sur la croix, Dieu lui a pardonné et  l'a sauvée de la punition qu'il méritait pour son péché.
  * Only God can save people from being punished for their sins.
  * Dieu seul peut sauver les gens de leur péché. 

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestion de traduction: =====

  * Ways to translate "save" could include, "deliver" or "rescue" or "pull away from danger."
  * Plusieurs façons de traduire "sauver" seraient "délivrer" ou "porter secours" ou "retirer du danger."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: Exemples tirés des histoires bibliques: =====

  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:09-08|[09-08]]]** Moses tried to **save**  his fellow Israelite. Moïse essaya de **porter secours** à son compatriote.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to **save**  the firstborn son of anyone who believed in him. Dieu donna un moyen de **sauver** le premier-né des familles qui croyaient en Lui.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]** Moses told the Israelites, "Stop being afraid! God will fight for you today and **save**  you." Moïse dit aux Israélites, “Cessez d'avoir peur! Dieu se battra pour vous aujourd'hui et il vous **sauvera**.”
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:12-13|[12-13]]]** The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to praise God because he **saved**  them from the Egyptian army. Les Israélites chantèrent plusieurs chansons pour célébrer leur nouvelle liberté et pour louer Dieu car il les avait **sauvés** de l'armée Égyptienne.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:16-17|[16-17]]]** This pattern repeated many times: the Israelites would sin, God would punish them, they would repent, and God would send a deliverer to **save**  them. Ce modèle se répéta plusieurs fois: les Israélites péchèrent, Dieu les punit, ils se repentirent, et Dieu leur envoya un sauveteur pour les** sauver**. 
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:44-08|[44-08]]]** "You crucified Jesus, but God raised him to life again! You rejected him, but there is no other way to be **saved**  except through the power of Jesus!" Vous l'avez crucifié, mais Dieu l'a ressuscité! Vous l'avez rejeté, mais c'est en Jésus seul que se trouve le salut!”
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:47-11|[47-11]]]** The jailer trembled as he came to Paul and Silas and asked, "What must I do to be **saved**?" Paul answered, "Believe in Jesus, the Master, and you and your family will be **saved**."
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-12|[49-12]]]** Good works cannot **save**  you.
  * **[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:49-13|[49-13]]]** God will **save**  everyone who believes in Jesus and receives him as their Master. But he will not **save**  anyone who does not believe in him.
