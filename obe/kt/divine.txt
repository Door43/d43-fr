====== divine/divin ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The term "divine" refers to anything pertaining to or related to God.

Le terme "divin" comprend tout ce qui est en rapport avec ou touche Dieu.

  * Some ways this term is used include, "divine authority," "divine judgment," and "divine nature," "divine power," and "divine glory."
  * Quelques emplois de ce mot comprend "autorité divine","jugement divin" et "nature divine," "puissance divine," et "gloire divine."

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestions de traduction: =====

  * When describing something, the term "divine" could also be translated as, "from God" or "pertaining to God" or "characterized by God."
  * Concernant la description de quelquechose, le terme "divin" pourrait se traduire par "de Dieu" ou "se rapportant à Dieu" ou caractérisé par Dieu."
  * The verb "to divine" something is a different word. It means to try to figure out things in the spirit world. It is related to the word, "divination."
  * Le verbe "deviner" est un mot différent. Sa définition est : essayer d'interpréter des choses dans le monde des esprits. C'est en relation avec le mot "divination."
