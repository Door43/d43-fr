====== confidence, confident/confiance, confiant ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

Confidence is the feeling of trust that comes from knowing that something is certain.

Confiance est avoir l'assurance d'être sûr de quelquechose.

  * Believers are confident in God's promises, knowing that he never lies and he always fulfills his word.
  * Les croyants mettent leur confiance dans les promesses de Dieu, sachant qu'Il ne ment jamais et accomplit toujours Sa parole.
  * Confidence that is based on trusting God will remain even when life's circumstances become extremely difficult.
  * L'assurance existe sur la base de notre confiance en Dieu et subsistera même quand les cirsconstances de la vie sont extrêmement difficilles.

(See also Voir aussi: [[:fr:obe:kt:trust|trust, trustworthy, trustworthiness]], [[:fr:obe:kt:hope|hope]], [[:fr:obe:kt:believe|believe, believe in, belief(croire,croire en,croyance)]])

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestions de traduction: =====

  * "To be confident" could also be translated as "to trust completely" or "to know without a doubt that something will happen."
  * "Avoir confiance" pourrait se traduire par "faire totalement confiance" ou "savoir sans aucun doute que la chose arrivera."
  * "Confidence" can be translated as "complete assurance" or "strong belief."
  * On pourrrait traduire "confiance" par "assurance totale" ou "ferme croyance."
