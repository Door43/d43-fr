====== adultery, adulterer, adulteress /adultère, un homme adultère, une femme adultère ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

Adultery is when a married person has sexual relations with someone who is not his spouse. An adulterer is a person who sins by committing adultery.

L'adultère est l'action d'une personne mariée ayant des relations sexuelles avec quelqu'un qui n'est pas son épouse (ou époux). Un ou une adultère  est une personne  qui péche commettant  l'adultère.

  * God commanded the Israelites to not commit adultery.
  * Dieu a commandé aux Israélite de ne pas commettre l'adultère.
  * The marriage relationship is the only context in which God approves sexual relations.
  * Les relations sexuelles sont uniquement acceptées par Dieu dans le cadre du mariage.
  * Adultery breaks the promises that a husband and wife made to each other in their covenant of marriage.
  * Commettre l'adultère dissout les promesses faites entre mari et femme lors de leur voeux de mariage.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * If the target language does not have one word that means "adultery," this term could be translated with a phrase such as, "having sexual relations with someone else's wife."
  * Some languages may have an indirect way of talking about adultery, such as "sleeping with someone else's spouse" or "being unfaithful to one's wife." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|euphemism]])
