====== forever/à jamais ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

In the Bible, the term "forever" refers to never-ending time.

Dans la Bible, le terme " jamais" se réfère au temps perpétuel.

  * The term "forever and ever" emphasizes that something will always exist.
  * L'expression "à jamais" met l'emphase sur quelquechose qui existera toujours.

* The phrase “forever and ever” is a way of expressing what eternity or eternal life is. It also has the idea of time that never ends.

L'expression "à jamais est une façon d'exprimer ce qu'est l'éternité ou la vie éternelle.

  * Sometimes in modern-day usage this word has a figurative meaning of "a very long time" as in, "We had to wait for him forever!" But this is not how it is used in the Bible.
  * Quelquefaois l'emploi moderne de ce mot a iun sens figuratif qui signifie " pendant très longtemps" comme dans " nous l'avons attendu ppour une éternité!" mais on ne l'uitklise pas ainsi dans la Bible.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could also be translated by "always" or "never ending."
  * The phrase, "will last forever" could also be translated as "always exist" or "will never stop" or "will always continue."
  * The emphatic phrase, "forever and ever" could also be translated as, "for always and always" or "not ever ending" or "which never, ever ends."
