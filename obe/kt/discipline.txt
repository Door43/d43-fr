====== discipline/discipline ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The term "discipline" refers to training people to obey a set of guidelines for moral behavior.

Le terme "discipline" signifie instruire des gens à obéir des règles de conduite morale.

  * Parents discipline their children by providing moral guidance and direction for them and teaching them to obey.
  * Des parents disciplinent  leurs enfants En les guidant moralement et en les instruisant à obéir.
  * Similarly, God disciplines his children to help them produce healthy spiritual fruit in their lives, such as joy, love, and patience.
  * De la même façon, Dieu discipline Ses enfants afin de les aider à produire de bons fruits spirituels tels que la joie, l'amour, et la patience.
  * Discipline involves instruction regarding how to live to please God, as well as punishment for behavior that is against God's will.
  * Discipline  comprend instruire comment vivre pour plaire Dieu, autant que punir à cause de notre conduite contraire à la volonté de Dieu.
  * Self-discipline is the process of applying moral and spiritual principles to one's own life.
  * Personnelle discipline est le fait de mettre en pratiquer des principes moraux et spirituels concernant notre propre vie.

===== Translation Suggestions: Suggestions de traduction: =====

  * Depending on the context, "discipline" could be translated as, "train and instruct" or "morally guide" or "punish for wrongdoing."
  * Selon le contexte, on pourrait traduire "discipline" par "élever et instruire" ou "guider moralement" ou "punir pour mauvaise conduite."
  * The noun, "discipline" could be translated as "moral training" or "punishment" or "moral correction" or "moral guidance and instruction."
  * On pourrait traduire le mot discipline par "instruction morale" ou "punition" ou "correction morale" ou " direction morale et instruction."
