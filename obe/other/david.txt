====== David(David) ======

====== les Faits: ======

David was the second king of Israel and he served God faithfully.

David a été le second roi d'Israël et servit dieu fidèlement.

  * God chose David to become king when he was still a young boy caring for his family's sheep.
  * Dieu choisit David pour être le roi d'Israël alors qu'il était un jeune garçon gardant les moutons de sa famille.
  * David became a great fighter and led the Israelite army in battles against their enemies.
  * David devint un grand guerrier et commanda l'armée d'Israël  durant les batailles contre leurs ennemis.
  * Saul tried to kill David, but God protected him, and made him king after Saul's death.
  * Saül essaya de tuer David, mais Dieu le protégea et le nomma roi d'Israël après la mort de Saül.
  * David sinned against God, but he repented and God forgave him.
  * David pécha envers Dieu, mais il se repentit et Dieu lui pardonna.
  * David's son, King Solomon, built the temple for God that David had planned.Jesus, the Messiah, is a descendent of David, and is therefore called the "Son of David."
  * Le fils de David, le roi Salomon, bâtit le temple pour Dieu dont David avait fait le plan. Jésus, le Messie, est un descendanrt de David, et fut donc appelé "le fils de David."

  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** God chose a young Israelite named **David** to be king after Saul. **David** was a shepherd from the town of Bethlehem. … **David** was a humble and righteous man who trusted and obeyed God. 
  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-03|[17-03]]]** **David** was also a great soldier and leader. When **David** was still a young man, he fought against a giant named Goliath. 
  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]** Saul became jealous of the people's love for **David**. Saul tried many times to kill him, so **David** hid from Saul. 
  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** God blessed **David** and made him successful. **David** fought many battles and God helped him defeat Israel's enemies.

  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]** **David** ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years, and God blessed him. However, toward the end of his life he sinned terribly against God. 
  ***[[:fr:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what **David** had done, so he sent the prophet Nathan to tell **David** how evil his sin was. **David** repented of his sin and God forgave him. For the rest of his life, **David** followed and obeyed God, even in difficult times.
