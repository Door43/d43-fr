===== Definition: Définition: =====

A eunuch is a man who has been castrated; though the term later came to simply mean a government official, even those who had not been castrated.

Un eunuque est un homme que l'on a castré; cepentant le terme plus tard simplemrent représente un officier governemental, même ceux qui n'ont pas subi la castration.

  * In ancient times, these men were often kings' servants who were set as guards over the women's quarters (as in the story of Esther).
  * Aux temps bibliques, ces hommes étaient souvent des serviteurs de rois en tant que gardiens des appartements des femmes (comme dans l'histoire d'Esther).
  * Some eunuchs were important government officials, as is probable in the New Testament account of the Ethiopian eunuch that Philip encountered.
  * Quelques eunnuques ont été des officiels gouvernementaux importants, comme probablement dans le Nouveau Testament, L'Ethiopien que Philippe a rencontré.
