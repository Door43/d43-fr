====== exile, the Exile/exiler, l'Exil ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The term "exile" means to force people to live somewhere away from their home country.

Le terme "exiler" signifie forcer des gens à vivre ailleurs loin de leur pays d'origine.

  * People are usually sent into exile for punishment or for political reasons.
  * Généralement, un peuple est envoyé en exil pour des raisons punitives ou politiques.
  * The "Exile" is a period in Bible history when many Jewish citizens of Jerusalem and the rest of the region of Judah were taken from their homes and forced to live in Babylon.
  * L' "Exil" est une période historique dans la Bible, durant laquelle beaucoup de citoyens juifs de Jérusalem et la région restante de Juda furent déportés et forcés de vivre à babylone.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "to exile" could also be translated as, "to send away" or "to force out" or "to banish."
  * The "Exile" could be translated with a word or phrase that means, "time of banishment" or "time of forced absence" or "banishment."
