====== Israel, Israelites, nation of Israel(Israël, Israélites, nation d'Israël) ======

====== les Faits: ======

Israel was the name God gave to Jacob. Israel means, "He struggles with God."

Israël était le nom que Dieu donna à Jacob et signifie " il lutte avec Dieu."

  * Jacob's descendants became known as the people of Israel, the nation of Israel, or the Israelites.
  * On connait les descendants de Jacob sous le nomde peuple d'Israël, la nation d'Israël ou les Israélites.
  * God formed his covenant with the people of Israel. They were his chosen people.
  * Dieu fit alliance avec le peuple d'Israël. Ils étaient son peuple choisi.
  * The Nation of Israel was made up of twelve tribes.
  * La nation d'Israël se composait de 12 tribus.
  *
  * Often the term "Israel" can be translated as, "the people of Israel" or "the nation of Israel," depending on the context.
