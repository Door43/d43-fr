====== defile, be defiled/souiller,être souillé ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The terms "defile" and "be defiled" refer to becoming polluted or dirty, especially in a moral sense in relation to God.

Les termes "souiller" et "être souillé" signifient devenir dégoûtant ou sale, spécialement dans un sens moral en relation avec Dieu.

  * God warned the Israelites to not defile themselves by eating or touching things that he had declared as "unclean" and "unholy."
  * Dieu avertit les Israélites de ne pas se souiller en mangeant ou en touchant des choses qu'Il déclara "impures" et "profanes."
  * God also gave them laws to follow that would help them avoid sexual sins that would "defile" them, making them unacceptable to God.
  * diue donna des lois à suivre pour les aider à éviter des péchés sexuels qui les "souilleraient", en étant intolérables pour Dieu.
  * In the New Testament, Jesus taught that it is a person's sinful thoughts and actions that defile him.
  * Dans le Nouveau Testament, Jésus enseigna que ce sont les pensées et actions pécheresses d'une personne qui le souillent.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "defile" can also be translated as "cause to be morally unclean" or "make unrighteous."
  * To "be defiled" could be translated as "become unclean" or "be morally unacceptable (to God)."
