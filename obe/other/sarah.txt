====== Sarah, Sarai(Sara,Saraï)  ======


===== Facts: les Faits: =====

    * Sarah was Abraham's wife.
    * Sara était la femme d'Abraham.
    * Sarah's name was originally "Sarai," but God changed it to Sarah.
    * A l'origine, le nom de Sara était "Saraï", mais Dieu le changea en Sara.
    * Sarah gave birth to the son God had promised to her and Abraham.
    * Sara donna naissance au fils que Dieu avait promis à Sara et à Abraham.
