====== camel/chameau ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: =====

A camel is a large, four legged animal with either one or two humps on its back.

un chameau est un grand animal de quatre pattes possédant duex bosses sur son dos.

  * In Bible times, the camel was the largest animal found in Israel and the surrounding regions.
  * Dans les temps bibliques, le chameau était le plus large animal en Israël et aux alentours.
  * The camel was used mainly for carrying people and burdens. Some people groups also used camels for food, but not the Israelites because God had said that camels were unclean and were not to be eaten.
  * On se servait du chameau pour transporter les gens et leurs bagages. Quelques groupes de gens mangeaient la viande du chameau, mais pas les Israélites, car Dieu leur avait dit que les chameaux étaient impurs et non consommables.
  * Camels were valuable because they could move swiftly in the sand and could live without food and water for several weeks at a time.
  * Les chameaux étaient précieux car il pouvaient se déplacer rapidement dans le sable et vivre sans manger ou boire pendant plusieurs semaines.
