===== Definition: Définition: =====

A banquet is large, formal meal that includes several courses.

Un banquet est un grand repas formel comprenant plusieurs services.

  * In ancient times, kings often served banquet meals to entertain political leaders and other important guests.
  * Autrefoois, les rois servaient des banquets pour divertir les leaders politiques et des invités importants.
  * This could also be translated as, "elaborate meal" or "important feast" or "multi-course meal."
  * La traduction pourrait être "repas élaboré" ou "un grand festin" ou "un repas à plusieurs services."
