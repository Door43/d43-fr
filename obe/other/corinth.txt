====== Corinth, Corinthians/Corinthe, Corinthiens ======

====== les Faits: ======

Corinth was a city in Greece about 50 miles west of Athens. The Corinthians were the people who lived at Corinth.

Corinthe était une ville en Grèce à environ 50 kilimètres d'Athènes.

  * Corinth was the location of one of the early Christian churches.
  * A Corinthe se trouvait l'une des premières églises chrétiennes.
  * The New Testament books, 1 Corinthians and 2 Corinthians were written to the church at Corinth.
  * Le Nouveau Testament contient 1Corinthiens et 2Corinthiens qui furent adressés à l'église de Corinthe.
  * On his first missionary journey, Paul stayed in Corinth for approximately 18 months.
  * Au cours de son premier voyage missionnaire Paul resta près de 18 mois à Corinthe.
  * Paul met Aquila and Priscilla while in Corinth.
  * Paul rencontre Priscille et Aquilas à Corinthe.
  * Other early church leaders associated with Corinth include Timothy, Titus, Apollos, and Silas.
  * A Corinthe sont aussi associés d'autres leaders des débuts de l'église tels que Timothée,Tite, Apollos, et  Silas.
