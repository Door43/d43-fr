====== Ammon, Ammonites / Ammi, Ammonites F ======

====== les Faits: ======

=====   =====

Ammon was the younger son of Lot by his younger daughter, and his descendants are called the Ammonites.

Ammi était le fils de Lot avec sa fille cadette, et ses descendants s'appellent les Ammonites.

  * The Ammonites lived east of the Jordan River and were hostile toward the Israelites.
  * Les Ammonites vivaient à l'Est du Jourdain et étaient hostiles envers les Israélites.
  * The Ammonites hired a prophet named Balaam to curse Israel, but God did not allow it.
  * Les Ammonites engagèrent un prophète du nom de Balaam pour maudire les Israélites, mais Dieu ne le permit pas.
