====== Amnon / Amnon ======

====== les Faits: ======

=====   =====

Amnon was the oldest son of King David and his wife Ahinoam.

Amnom était le fils aîné du roi David et de sa femme Ahinoam.

  * Amnon raped his half-sister Tamar, who was also Absalom's sister.
  * Amnon déshonora sa demi-soeur Tamar, qui était la soeur d'Absalom, en couchant avec elle.
  * Because of this, Absalom plotted against Amnon and had him killed.
  * A cause de cela, Absalom complota contre Amnon et le fit tuer.
