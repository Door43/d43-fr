====== creature/créature ======

====== les Faits: ======  

===== Definition: Définition: =====

The term "creature" refers generally to all the living beings that God created, both humans and animals.

Le terme "créature" se réfère à tous les êtres vivants que Dieu a créé, humains et animaux.

  * The prophet Ezekiel described seeing "living creatures" in his vision of the glory of God. He did not know what they were, so he gave them a very general label.
  * Le prophète Ezéchiel  décrivit sa vision des "créatures vivantes" lorsque qu'il eut sa vision de la gloire de Dieu. Ne sachant pas ce que c'était, il a employé des termes généraux dans leur description.

====== Translation Suggestions ======

  * Depending on the context, this term could be translated as, "living being" or "created being" or "everything God created" or "people and animals" or "animals" or "human beings."
